Dopamine dysregulation contributes to anxiety. Low dopamine impairs cognitive control and threat-processing in the prefrontal cortex. MIF-1's dopamine enhancement may reduce anxiety by improving emotional regulation and reducing fear-based cognition. Users report improved anxiolytic effects when combined with magnesium and breathwork protocols. Results typically appear by week 2-3.
