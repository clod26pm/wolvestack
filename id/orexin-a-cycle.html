Orexin-A Cycling Protocols

Dosing Schedules & Tolerance Management

Orexin-A cycling typically follows 2-4 weeks on (5-20 mcg daily) followed by 1-2 weeks off to maintain sensitivity. Common protocols: 3 weeks on/10 days off, or 4 weeks on/14 days off. During off weeks, sleep patterns reset and receptor sensitivity recovers. Off-cycle effects (increased sleep pressure) are expected and beneficial.
