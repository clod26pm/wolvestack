Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All your dose logs, protocols, and settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to a server. If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed.

How do I restore a WolveStack Pro purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." This will sync your subscription status with the App Store. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings on your iPhone, tap your name, then Subscriptions, and select WolveStack to cancel or modify your plan.

The app is crashing or not loading properly

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the issue persists, make sure you're running the latest version from the App Store. If it still doesn't work, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll investigate.

Can I request a new peptide be added to the library?

Yes! Email us at wolvestack@pm.me with the peptide name and any relevant details. We regularly update the library based on user requests.

Is WolveStack available on Android?

Not yet. We're currently iOS-only but considering Android in the future.
