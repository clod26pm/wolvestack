WolveStack App Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

WolveStack ("the App") is a peptide research tracking application for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Peptide dose logs you create (peptide name, dose, timestamp, notes)
  • Custom protocols and stacks you build
  • App preferences and settings
  • Onboarding completion status

This data is stored entirely on your device using local storage. We do not have access to it and it is not transmitted to any server.

Subscription and Purchase Data

If you purchase a subscription or one-time upgrade, the transaction is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives:

  • An anonymous app user ID
  • Purchase and subscription status from Apple
  • Device platform (iOS)

RevenueCat does not receive your name, email, or Apple ID. For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Analytics

The App does not include any analytics SDKs. We do not track your usage, screen views, or behavior within the App.

Push Notifications

If you enable dose reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App may contain links to external vendor websites. These websites have their own privacy policies which we do not control. We encourage you to review their policies before providing any personal information.

Email Communications

If you choose to provide your email address (for example, by opting in to updates within the App), we may use it to send you product updates, educational content, and occasionally curated third-party offers relevant to the peptide research community. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time via the unsubscribe link in any email. We will never sell your email address to third parties.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties. The only external service that receives any data is RevenueCat for purchase verification, as described above. If you opt in to email communications, your email address may be stored in our email service provider for the sole purpose of sending you the communications described above.

Data Deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the App. No additional steps are required.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
