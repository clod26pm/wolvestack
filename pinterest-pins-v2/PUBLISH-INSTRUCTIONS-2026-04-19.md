## How to Publish Manually
1. Go to https://www.pinterest.com/pin-builder/?tab=save_from_url
2. Paste the **Image URL** and submit
3. Click the image thumbnail → Click "Add 1 Pin"
4. Set title, then change destination link from image URL to article **Link**
5. Type description LAST (it loses content if you click away)
6. Click Publish

## Alternative: Direct Upload
If pin images can be uploaded directly from local disk:
1. Go to https://www.pinterest.com/pin-builder/
2. Drag and drop the PNG from `peptide-daily-content/pinterest-pins-v2/`
3. Fill in Title, Description, Link, and Board
4. Click Publish
