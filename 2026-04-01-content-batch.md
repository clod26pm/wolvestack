While extremely small (n=2) and preliminary, this is the first published human IV safety data for BPC-157 and is generating significant discussion in the biohacker community. A separate systematic review on BPC-157 in orthopaedic sports medicine (PMC12313605) and a narrative review titled "Regeneration or Risk?" (PMC12446177) were also published recently.
