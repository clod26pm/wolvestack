Research suggests diminishing returns after 8 weeks of continuous dosing. This is why cycling protocols (8-12 weeks on, 4-6 weeks off) are recommended. Some users continue beyond 8 weeks if still experiencing improvements, but this is the exception rather than the rule. After the 8-week window, tissue typically maximizes its "repair response" to the peptide, though healing continues post-cycle. Taking breaks allows for receptor sensitivity reset.

Most experts recommend the moderate 500 mcg standard unless you have severe injury requiring aggressive protocol, in which case 1000 mcg split dosing is considered acceptable.
