Frequently Asked Questions About FOXO4-DRI

What is FOXO4-DRI?

FOXO4-DRI (FOXO4 D-Retro-Inverso peptide) is a Senolytic peptide. Synthetic peptide designed to disrupt FOXO4-p53 protein-protein interaction in senescent cells, with D-retro-inverso modification for protease resistance. It is researched for senescent cell elimination, improved tissue function, cartilage regeneration, testosterone restoration, anti-aging.

What is the recommended FOXO4-DRI dosage?

Common dosages range from 5-10 mg per dose, administered 3 consecutive days per cycle via subcutaneous or intravenous injection. Cycles typically run 3-day dosing periods, repeated every 4-6 weeks for 16 weeks total.

What are the side effects of FOXO4-DRI?

Minimal side effects in preclinical studies. Potential transient inflammation during senescent cell clearance as cellular debris triggers immune response. No human safety data published.

Is FOXO4-DRI legal?

Not FDA-approved. Research use only.
