Frequently Asked Questions

At what age should I start sermorelin for anti-aging?

GH decline accelerates after 40. Starting sermorelin at 40-45 is optimal. Benefits are larger in 50+ individuals where GH decline is pronounced. Younger individuals (30-39) see modest benefits.

How much skin improvement can I expect?

Expect +20-30% improvement in skin elasticity, reduced fine lines, improved skin tone and hydration. Results take 8-12 weeks and continue improving through 6 months.

Can sermorelin prevent or reverse osteoporosis?

Sermorelin increases bone density +2-4% in 12 weeks, supporting osteoporosis prevention. It's not a replacement for calcium/vitamin D/exercise but synergizes with them.

Should older adults use different sermorelin doses?

Yes. Older adults (55+) benefit from slightly higher doses (750-1000 mcg) due to lower baseline GH. Taper more conservatively to avoid joint pain sensitivity.

How long do anti-aging results last?

Improvements plateau after 12-16 weeks. Discontinuation gradually reverses results over 8-12 weeks. Maintenance cycles every 5-6 months sustain anti-aging benefits.

What other peptides support anti-aging with sermorelin?

IGF-1 LR3, BPC-157, and NAD-boosting peptides stack synergistically. GHK-Cu supports collagen synthesis independent of GH axis.
