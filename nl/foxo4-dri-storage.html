How to Store FOXO4-DRI

Temperature, Shelf Life & Stability

📖 5 min read 🔬 Research & Education ⚠️ Not medical advice ✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Medical Disclaimer

Store lyophilized FOXO4-DRI powder at 2-8°C (refrigerator) in original sealed vial, protected from light. Unopened vials remain stable 6-12 months or longer if stored properly. Reconstituted solution is stable 2-4 weeks at 2-8°C. Never freeze lyophilized peptide (freezing damages structure). Avoid room temperature or warm storage (accelerates degradation). Proper storage maximizes potency and safety.

Storage Conditions for Lyophilized FOXO4-DRI Powder

FOXO4-DRI arrives as lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder in sealed vials. Optimal storage: 2-8°C in a standard refrigerator, in original sealed packaging, protected from light. Standard refrigerator temperature (4-5°C) is ideal. Lyophilized peptides are remarkably stable if stored correctly—vials often remain viable 6-12 months, sometimes longer depending on specific synthesis batch and storage conditions.

What Damages Lyophilized FOXO4-DRI?

Heat: High temperatures (>8°C) accelerate water uptake and chemical degradation. Room temperature (20-25°C) storage for more than a few days significantly reduces potency. Hot environments (35°C+) degrade peptide within days to weeks.

Freezing: Counterintuitively, freezing at 0°C or below can damage lyophilized peptides. Ice crystal formation causes physical stress on the peptide molecule, potentially causing denaturation. Never freeze unopened FOXO4-DRI vials. This is a critical mistake to avoid.

Light: UV light can damage peptides through photochemical degradation. Store in original opaque vials, not in clear containers. Keep vials in a dark area of the refrigerator if possible.

Moisture: Lyophilized peptides are sensitive to moisture. Keep vials sealed. Don't open vials unnecessarily. Store in a refrigerator with moderate (not extremely humid) conditions—high humidity can degrade the powder even in sealed vials.

Contamination: Airborne bacteria, dust, or fungal spores can contaminate opened vials. Minimize exposure during reconstitution by using aseptic technique.

Shipping and Receipt: Ensuring Intact Powder

Upon receipt, inspect vials for: intact seal (should not be open or loosened), absence of visible discoloration in the powder (should be white or off-white), and proper packaging (vials should arrive in insulated boxes with ice packs to maintain cold). If vials arrive warm, partially thawed, opened, or discolored, contact the vendor immediately. Compromised peptide from poor shipping may be less potent or contaminated. Once received, immediately place in refrigerator—don't leave at room temperature.

Storage Duration: How Long Does FOXO4-DRI Last?

Unopened lyophilized FOXO4-DRI stored properly at 2-8°C remains stable approximately 6-12 months from manufacture date. Some vendors claim longer stability (18-24 months) if vials are stored in extreme conditions (0-5°C, minimized light exposure). However, most conservative approach: assume 12-month stability and use within that timeframe. Check the manufacture date on the vial when received; if stored for 3-6 months before you receive it, plan to use it within 6-9 months of receipt.

Reconstituted Solution Storage

Once reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, FOXO4-DRI solution is far less stable than powder. Store reconstituted solution at 2-8°C and use within 2-4 weeks. The benzyl alcohol preservative in bacteriostatic water extends shelf life compared to regular water (which would degrade in days). After 4 weeks, discard any remaining reconstituted solution—don't use expired peptide. Mark the vial with reconstitution date immediately after mixing to avoid accidentally using old solution.

Container Type: Glass Vials vs. Plastic

FOXO4-DRI is supplied in glass vials with rubber stoppers, which is ideal for long-term peptide storage. Glass is inert and doesn't leach compounds into the peptide. If you must transfer peptide (not recommended), use amber glass vials only—never plastic containers, which can leach compounds and may not provide adequate barrier to light/moisture.

Travel and Portable Storage

If traveling, transport unopened FOXO4-DRI vials in insulated travel cases with cooling packs to maintain 2-8°C during transit. Don't leave vials in hot cars or direct sunlight, even briefly. Upon arrival at destination, immediately refrigerate. Once reconstituted, transport reconstituted solutions only if absolutely necessary (short-term trip), keeping vials in coolers or insulated bags with ice packs. Don't carry peptides across international borders—legal and practical complications make this inadvisable.

Signs of Peptide Degradation

How to tell if lyophilized peptide has degraded: visible discoloration (yellowing, browning, darkening), clumping or caking of powder (should be fine, uniform powder), strong odor (fresh peptide is nearly odorless), or appearance of liquid or moisture in sealed vial (indicates seal failure). Reconstituted peptide degradation: cloudiness or visible particles in solution (should be clear), discoloration, or off-odor. If any degradation signs are present, discard the peptide—don't use it.

Vial Integrity: When Rubber Stoppers Fail

The rubber stopper can deteriorate over extended storage or with repeated needle punctures. If the stopper becomes visibly degraded, the vial's seal is compromised. Once a vial has been punctured multiple times (more than 5-10 times), consider it potentially compromised—the stopper becomes less effective, allowing air and moisture infiltration. For multiple-use vials, minimize punctures and use within 1-2 weeks after first opening.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it okay to store FOXO4-DRI in a freezer?

No. Freezing damages lyophilized peptides. Store at refrigerator temperature (2-8°C) only, never at 0°C or below.

How do I know if my FOXO4-DRI went bad?

Visible discoloration, clumping, moisture in vial, or strong odor indicates degradation. Reconstituted solution that appears cloudy or discolored should be discarded.

Can I keep FOXO4-DRI at room temperature temporarily?

Briefly (hours) is okay for travel. Prolonged room temperature storage (days) significantly reduces potency. Always refrigerate as soon as possible.

Does FOXO4-DRI expire?

Lyophilized: ~12 months from manufacture. Reconstituted: 2-4 weeks. After these periods, assume potency degradation and discard.

Can I extend storage life by keeping FOXO4-DRI in a frost-free freezer?

Frost-free freezers cycle above freezing temperatures, defeating the purpose of freezing. This would actually increase degradation. Stick with standard refrigerator storage.

What if my vial seal was slightly compromised during shipping?

If the seal appears intact but you're concerned, assume integrity is compromised—use the peptide sooner rather than later (within 1-2 weeks). If seal is obviously broken, the peptide is likely contaminated or degraded—discard.
