NAD+ for Men

Benefits, Dosing & Protocol

NAD+ supports male hormone balance, muscle recovery, and sexual function through mitochondrial optimization. Protocols using 500-1,000 mg daily NMN/NR combined with resistance training improve testosterone signaling, erectile function, muscle composition, and cardiovascular health within 8-12 weeks.

NAD+ and Testosterone Signaling

NAD+-dependent sirtuins regulate testosterone synthesis and androgen receptor sensitivity in male tissues. SIRT1 activation enhances mitochondrial function in Leydig cells, improving testosterone production. Low NAD+ impairs hormone synthesis, reducing libido and muscle gain capacity.

NAD+ restoration improves testosterone signaling without directly elevating levels. Users report improved libido, better muscle gain potential, and enhanced sexual function within 4-8 weeks. Combined with resistance training and zinc, NAD+ maximizes testosterone-driven anabolic effects.

NAD+ and Erectile Function

Erectile dysfunction involves endothelial dysfunction and impaired nitric oxide production—both rooted in mitochondrial dysfunction. NAD+ restoration improves endothelial function, enhances vasodilation, and increases nitric oxide bioavailability. Users report improved erectile rigidity and sexual performance within 4-6 weeks.

Stack NAD+ 500 mg + L-citrulline 3-5g + L-arginine 2-3g for comprehensive erectile support. Results are often dramatic—users report marked improvement by week 4-6. This combination addresses energy production (NAD+) and vasodilation (amino acids).

NAD+ for Muscle Growth and Recovery

NAD+ supports muscle protein synthesis through mTOR pathway activation and improved mitochondrial energy capacity. Muscles recover faster from resistance training when NAD+ is optimized. Users report reduced DOMS, faster strength gains, and improved muscle definition within 4-8 weeks when combined with training.

Optimal male protocol for muscle: NAD+ 750 mg + creatine 5g + resistance training 4-5x/week. Creatine supports phosphocreatine energy; NAD+ optimizes mitochondrial ATP production. Together, this maximizes muscle gain capacity in middle-aged and older men.

Cardiovascular and Prostate Health

Cardiovascular aging involves endothelial dysfunction, arterial stiffness, and mitochondrial dysfunction—all addressable by NAD+. Blood pressure improves, blood flow increases, and arrhythmia risk decreases. NAD+ also supports prostate mitochondrial function, potentially reducing BPH symptoms.

Older men using NAD+ 500 mg daily report improved exercise capacity, better blood pressure, and reduced urinary hesitation within 8-12 weeks. Combine with L-citrulline, CoQ10, and cardiovascular exercise for comprehensive vascular anti-aging.

NAD+ and Male Fertility

Sperm mitochondria are essential for motility and fertilization capacity. Low NAD+ impairs mitochondrial function, reducing sperm quality. NAD+ restoration improves mitochondrial function in sperm-producing cells, enhancing motility, morphology, and fertilization capacity.

Men optimizing fertility should use NAD+ 500 mg daily combined with CoQ10 300 mg, selenium 200mcg, and zinc 25-30 mg. Results manifest over 8-12 weeks (spermatogenesis cycle). Partner studies show improved conception rates with this combined protocol.

Male-Specific NAD+ Stack

Comprehensive male longevity: NAD+ 750 mg + testosterone-support (zinc 30 mg) + sexual function (L-citrulline 5g) + muscle (creatine 5g) + cardiovascular (CoQ10 200 mg). This addresses hormones, sexual function, muscle, and heart—core male priorities.

Cycle 12 weeks on, 1-2 weeks off. Combine with resistance training 4-5x/week, adequate sleep, and reduced stress. Men report remarkable improvements: improved body composition, better recovery, enhanced sexual function, clearer thinking.

NAD+ and Aging in Men

Male aging accelerates after 40 due to NAD+ decline and declining hormone production. Restoring NAD+ addresses root cause (mitochondrial dysfunction) while complementary compounds support hormone pathways. Men often see dramatic anti-aging benefits: improved body composition, better recovery, enhanced sexual function.

Older men (50+) using NAD+-based protocols report feeling and performing like they did 10-15 years prior. Consistency is key—benefits plateau after 12 weeks but continue indefinitely with maintenance supplementation.
