Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will sermorelin improve my sleep?

Sleep improvements often appear by week 2-3, making sleep one of the earliest detectable benefits. Improvements continue through week 8-12 and plateau thereafter.

Should I inject sermorelin right before bed?

Inject 30-60 minutes before bed to align with natural sleep onset. Injecting too close to bed causes injection site discomfort before sleep.

Can sermorelin help with sleep apnea?

Sermorelin may worsen sleep apnea in some individuals due to increased muscle relaxation. Use caution in diagnosed sleep apnea; consult healthcare provider.

Can I combine sermorelin with sleep aids?

Yes. Sermorelin synergizes with melatonin, magnesium, and glycine. Avoid heavy sedatives that suppress GH secretion. Light sleep support enhances sermorelin benefits.

How much deeper will my sleep become?

Expect +20-30% increase in deep sleep duration (adding 10-20 minutes of delta-wave sleep to typical 8-hour night). REM sleep consolidation improves dream recall and mood.

Does sermorelin help with insomnia?

Sermorelin is effective for sleep maintenance and depth but less effective for sleep onset issues due to poor absorption. Combine with sleep hygiene optimization for best results.
