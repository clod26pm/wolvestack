N-Acetyl Selank Amidate Reconstitution

Powder Preparation, Dilution & Solution Stability

📖 9 min read🔬 Research & Education⚠️ Not medical advice✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Medical Disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. Not FDA-approved for human use. Consult a licensed healthcare professional. See full disclaimer.

N-Acetyl Selank Amidate powder reconstitution: dissolve in bacteriostatic saline (typically 250 mcg per mL). Use sterile technique, maintain refrigeration, and verify pH. Properly reconstituted solutions remain stable 2-4 weeks refrigerated or 1-2 weeks at room temperature.
