How to Store ARA-290

Temperature, Shelf Life & Stability

📖 5 min read 🔬 Research & Education ⚠️ Not medical advice ✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Medical Disclaimer

ARA-290 lyophilized powder should be stored at 2-8°C (refrigerated) or -20°C (frozen) in a light-protected container. Reconstituted solution is stable for approximately 1-2 weeks refrigerated at 2-8°C. Proper storage maintains potency and prevents degradation from heat, light, and moisture exposure.

ARA-290 Storage Requirements

Like all peptides, ARA-290 is susceptible to degradation from heat, light, moisture, and freeze-thaw cycles. Proper storage is critical for maintaining potency and efficacy throughout the intended use period.

Storage Temperature

  • Lyophilized Powder (Unopened): Store at 2-8°C (35-46°F), or preferably -20°C (-4°F)
  • Reconstituted Solution (in water or saline): Store at 2-8°C (35-46°F); do not freeze reconstituted peptide
  • Room Temperature (Avoid): Do not store at room temperature (~20-25°C); degradation accelerates significantly

Light Protection

ARA-290 should be stored in amber or opaque containers, or wrapped in foil to exclude light. Peptide bonds are photosensitive and degrade under visible and UV light exposure. Keep vials in original packaging when not in immediate use, or store in light-proof boxes or bags.

Humidity Control

Moisture accelerates peptide degradation. Store lyophilized powder in a low-humidity environment. Pharmaceutical-grade desiccant packs in original containers help maintain dryness. Avoid bathrooms and other high-humidity areas.

Home Storage Best Practices

Refrigerator Storage (Most Common)

  • Use a dedicated refrigerator shelf or designated peptide storage box (not shared with food)
  • Maintain consistent temperature: 2-8°C (use refrigerator thermometer to verify)
  • Avoid frequent temperature fluctuations from opening/closing refrigerator
  • Keep away from the refrigerator back (coldest area prone to ice formation)
  • Store in original packaging or light-proof container
  • Position away from the door (temperature fluctuates more)

Freezer Storage (Extended Stability)

  • -20°C freezer (standard home freezer) is suitable for long-term powder storage
  • Allows storage for 1-2+ years without significant degradation
  • Use once and discard approach: do not repeatedly thaw and refreeze
  • Wrap vials in foil to block light and protect from freezer burn
  • Thaw at room temperature or in refrigerator before use; do not use hot water

Reconstitution & Liquid Storage

Reconstitution Process

ARA-290 powder is typically supplied in vials requiring reconstitution with sterile water for injection (SWFI) or 0.9% saline solution. The reconstitution ratio depends on the vial contents (e.g., 2mg, 4mg):

  • Standard approach: Reconstitute to 1 mg/mL concentration (e.g., 2mg vial + 2mL SWFI = 1 mg/mL)
  • Use sterile, aseptic technique throughout
  • Allow 5-10 minutes for complete dissolution at room temperature
  • Invert gently (do not shake vigorously, which denatures peptides)

Reconstituted Solution Stability

  • Refrigerated (2-8°C): 7-14 days in SWFI or saline
  • Room Temperature (20-25°C): 2-4 hours (use immediately after reconstitution when possible)
  • Frozen (-20°C): Not recommended; freeze-thaw cycles degrade peptides

Sterility Maintenance After Reconstitution

Once reconstituted, maintain sterile conditions:

  • Store in sterile vials or syringes
  • Use sterile needles for each withdrawal
  • Alcohol-swab the rubber septum before each puncture
  • Discard reconstituted solution if there are any signs of contamination (cloudiness, discoloration, particulates)
  • Do not share reconstituted vials between users

ARA-290 Shelf Life

Unopened Powder Vials

  • Refrigerated (2-8°C): 18-24 months from manufacture date
  • Frozen (-20°C): 2-3+ years from manufacture date
  • Always check the manufacturer's expiration date on the vial label
  • Stability assumes proper storage conditions throughout distribution and use

Reconstituted Solutions

  • Refrigerated: 7-14 days (use conservative estimate of 7 days)
  • Room temperature: 2-4 hours maximum
  • Write the date and time of reconstitution on the vial for tracking

Pre-filled Syringes

Some suppliers provide ARA-290 in ready-to-use pre-filled syringes (reconstituted solution). Storage:

  • Refrigerate at 2-8°C until use
  • Use within 7-14 days of receipt or per manufacturer guidance
  • Do not freeze pre-filled syringes

Signs of ARA-290 Degradation

Inspect stored ARA-290 periodically. Discard if any of these signs are present:

Lyophilized Powder

  • Discoloration: Yellow, brown, or pink tint (fresh powder is white or off-white)
  • Clumping: Moisture absorption causes aggregation
  • Crystalline deposits: Suggests moisture exposure
  • Odor changes: Unusual or sour smell indicates contamination

Reconstituted Solution

  • Cloudiness: Protein aggregation or particulates
  • Discoloration: Any color tint beyond clear
  • Precipitate: Visible particles settling
  • Odor: Unusual or sour smell suggests bacterial growth
  • Mold or visible growth: Discard immediately

International Shipping & Storage Considerations

ARA-290 sourced internationally may arrive under various storage conditions. Upon receipt:

  • Inspect the package for signs of temperature abuse (melting, crystallization)
  • Check the vial itself for condensation, leakage, or discoloration
  • Verify the expiration date accounts for shipping and handling time
  • Transfer immediately to proper storage (2-8°C refrigerator or -20°C freezer)
  • If shipped during warm months, request overnight or 2-day expedited shipping with ice packs

Safe Disposal of ARA-290

Expired or degraded ARA-290 should be disposed of responsibly:

  • Pharmaceutical Take-Back Programs: Most pharmacies accept expired peptides and medications at no cost
  • Household Hazardous Waste: Check local waste management guidelines for peptide disposal
  • Not in Regular Trash: Avoid disposing of peptides in municipal garbage (environmental considerations)
  • Not in Sewer: Do not flush down the drain (peptides degrade in aquatic environments)
  • Sharps Disposal: If disposing of needles, use sharps containers per local regulations

ARA-290 Storage During Travel

Domestic Travel

  • Cooler with ice packs: Maintain 2-8°C during travel
  • Duration: Up to 24-48 hours safely with adequate insulation and ice
  • Keep sealed: Prevent condensation buildup inside the cooler

International Travel

  • Regulatory check: Verify ARA-290's legal status in the destination country before traveling
  • Original packaging: Keep in original labeled vials for customs
  • Cooler case: Travel-sized insulated cases with cooling packs maintain temperature
  • Pharmaceutical documentation: Carry any available documentation showing research/educational use
  • Declare at customs: Be prepared to declare peptide possession to customs authorities

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I store ARA-290 at room temperature?
Not recommended. Room temperature storage causes rapid degradation. Shelf life drops from months to days. Always refrigerate at 2-8°C or freeze at -20°C.
How long does reconstituted ARA-290 last?
Refrigerated reconstituted solution lasts 7-14 days. Use the conservative 7-day estimate. Room temperature solution lasts only 2-4 hours—use immediately after mixing.
Is it okay to refreeze thawed ARA-290?
No. Freeze-thaw cycles damage peptide structure through ice crystal formation. Once thawed, use the reconstituted solution within 7-14 days refrigerated. Do not refreeze.
What temperature should my refrigerator be for ARA-290 storage?
2-8°C (35-46°F). Use a refrigerator thermometer to verify temperature. Avoid storing in the coldest section (back) where freezing can occur.
Can light damage my ARA-290?
Yes. Peptides are photosensitive. Store in amber vials or wrap in foil to exclude light. Even refrigerator light can contribute to degradation over time.
How do I know if my ARA-290 has degraded?
Powder: discoloration (yellowing), clumping, crystalline deposits. Solution: cloudiness, precipitate, discoloration, unusual odor. When in doubt, discard.
Should I use hot water or room temperature water to reconstitute?
Room temperature water only. Hot water denatures peptides. Room temperature or slightly cool water is ideal. Allow 5-10 minutes for dissolution; avoid vigorous shaking.
