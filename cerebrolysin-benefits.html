What Is Cerebrolysin?

Cerebrolysin (Enzymatically-treated porcine brain peptide extract) is a Multi-peptide neuroprotective compound. Extract of pig brain containing bioavailable neurotrophic factors including BDNF, GDNF, NGF, and CNTF.

The only multi-peptide neuroprotective compound approved for clinical use in Europe, with the largest body of human clinical trial data of any cognitive peptide. It has attracted significant research interest for its potential effects on cognitive improvement, neuroprotection, stroke recovery, traumatic brain injury recovery, dementia symptom reduction, memory improvement.

How Does Cerebrolysin Produce These Benefits?

Crosses the blood-brain barrier and promotes neurogenesis, neuroplasticity, and neuroprotection through endogenous neurotrophic signaling. Contains bioavailable BDNF and GDNF-like factors that directly support neural growth and synaptic function, enhance neuronal survival, and reduce apoptosis.

This multi-pathway activity is why Cerebrolysin shows potential across several different applications rather than being limited to a single use case.

Can Cerebrolysin Help With Cognitive Improvement?

Research suggests Cerebrolysin may support cognitive improvement through its multi-peptide neuroprotective compound activity. Randomized double-blind trials up to 28 weeks in Alzheimer's disease showed superiority to placebo on cognitive measures. Benefits also demonstrated in vascular dementia. 20+ clinical trials completed with thousands of subjects. Approved in Europe and Asia for neurological conditions.

Protocols targeting cognitive improvement typically use 5-30 mL daily administered once daily for 4-28 weeks depending on indication.
