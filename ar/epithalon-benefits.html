How Do XTERMX00 Benefits Compare to Retinoids or Other Anti-Aging Treatments?

Epithalon mechanisms differ from retinoids: retinoids work through vitamin A receptor activation and increased cellular turnover; Epithalon works through telomerase and collagen enhancement. Epithalon هو ألطف (التهيج الأدنى)، ليس لديه مشاكل حساسية للأشعة فوق البنفسجية، ولا يسبب إعادة القصدير (فترة تسوية الجلد). Some users combine Epithalon with retinoids for synergistic benefits, though this is not formally studied.
