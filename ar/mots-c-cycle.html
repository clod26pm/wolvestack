MOTS-c cycling protocol: 8-12 week cycles at 5-10mg weekly via injection, followed by 8-10 week breaks. Cycles can be repeated 2-3 times yearly. This pattern prevents tolerance, allows receptor resensitization, and optimizes sustained metabolic benefits over months and years.
