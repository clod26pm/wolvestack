حالات سوء السلوك

Mistake: Shaking the vial strongly after added water (denatures peptide) —use only gentle swirling. Mistake: Injecting water directly onto the powder pile (cause clumping)-always inject against the vial wall. Mistake: Using non-sterile or contaminated water (introduces bacteria)-always use verified sterile, bacteriostatic water. Mistake: Using hot water (denatures peptide)-use room-temperature water only. سوء التصرف: عدم وضع علامة على القارورة مع تاريخ إعادة البناء (لا يمكن تتبع الطازجة) Mistake: Storing reconstituted peptide at room temperature (degrades in days) -always refrigerate at 2-8°C.
