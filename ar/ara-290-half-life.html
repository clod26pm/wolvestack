The short 20-30 minute serum half-life creates important pharmacokinetic consequences:

  • No meaningful drug accumulation with daily dosing:By 24 hours post-injection, serum XTERMX00 concentrations return to near-baseline, preventing steady-state accumulation even with repeated daily dosing. This contrasts with longer-acting peptides (e.g., BPC-157, which has a curriculum half-life of several days).
  • ولا تزال تركيزات القضايا أعلى من المصل:Despite low serum levels beyond 24 hours, receptor-bound XTERMX00 and tissue-associated peptide can maintain bioactivity. وهذا هو السبب في أن الجرعة اليومية مرة واحدة كافية على الرغم من قصر المصل نصف العمر.
