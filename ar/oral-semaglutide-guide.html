Quick Answer

Oral semaglutide (Rybelsus) is a GLP-1 agonist taken by mouth using patented SNAC technology for absorption enhancement. While bioavailability is significantly lower than injectable formulations (approximately 1% vs 89%), oral semaglutide offers convenience and addresses needle-phobia concerns. The 2025-2026 introduction of 25mg and 50mg formulations has improved efficacy, making oral semaglutide a viable option for weight loss management alongside traditional injectable GLP-1s like semaglutide and tirzepatide.
