الخط الزمني الضائع

Week 1-3: minimal visible fat loss; appetite increases counteracting any modest lipolytic effect. Week 4-6: fat loss becomes apparent despite elevated appetite; combination of lipolysis + nutrient partitioning + increased training overwhelms appetite-driven caloric excess. Week 6-10: maximum fat loss rate achieved; body composition visibly improves (muscle gain + fat loss = definition improvement). Week 10-12: fat loss rate slows as cycle approach plateau; minimal additional fat loss without further caloric deficit.
