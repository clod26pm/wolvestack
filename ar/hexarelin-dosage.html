Hexarelin Dosage Guide

Recommended Protocol & Cycle Length

Hexarelin typical dosage: 50-200 mcg per injection, 1-3 times daily. Beginners start 50-100 mcg once daily; advanced users employ 150-200 mcg 2-3 times daily. Total daily doses range 100-600 mcg depending on experience and tolerance. Cycle length: 8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off. Doses above 200 mcg per injection show diminishing returns with proportionally greater side effects.

Beginner Dosing

New users start 50-100 mcg once daily for 3-5 days to assess tolerance and GH response. This conservative approach detects side effects at minimal dose before escalating. Most beginners experience adequate GH elevation at 100-150 mcg once daily. After 3-5 days, increase to twice-daily injections (morning/afternoon) at 100 mcg each if tolerating well. This progression allows gradual acclimatization while monitoring tolerance. Beginners often achieve excellent results with 100-150 mcg twice daily (200-300 mcg total daily) without requiring three daily injections.

Intermediate Dosing

Users with previous GH secretagogue experience typically tolerate hexarelin 150-200 mcg twice daily (300-400 mcg total daily). Starting dose: 150 mcg once daily for 3-5 days, then escalate to 150-200 mcg twice daily on empty stomach spaced 6-8 hours apart. This dual-injection protocol produces sustained GH elevation throughout waking day while avoiding excessive receptor stimulation. Some intermediate users add third dose (50-100 mcg evening) if strength/size gains plateau after 4-6 weeks, providing enhanced GH stimulation for final 6-8 weeks of cycle.

Advanced Dosing

Experienced users with excellent tolerance employ 150-200 mcg three times daily (450-600 mcg total daily). This high-frequency protocol maximizes GH elevation across 24-hour cycle, producing most aggressive anabolic response. However, this dose range demands excellent management of appetite suppression, cortisol monitoring, and sleep quality. Users at this dose often implement 1-2 days off per week or pulse-cycling to mitigate receptor desensitization. Advanced protocols may add adjunctive compounds or support compounds (vitamin B6, zinc, magnesium) to enhance response.

Dose Optimization

Optimal dose is individual and requires personal assessment. Start low, assess response over 3-5 days, then escalate if tolerated. Most users achieve peak results in 150-200 mcg twice daily range; doses above this show diminishing returns. Individual GH responsiveness varies 2-3 fold (some achieve robust response at 100 mcg, others require 300 mcg). Users unable to tolerate 150-200 mcg due to side effects should consider GHRP-6 or ipamorelin alternatives producing milder but still substantial GH elevation.

Timing and Spacing

Optimal hexarelin timing maximizes GH response while avoiding circadian disruption. Morning injection (6-7 AM): supports increased metabolism/nutrient partitioning throughout morning. Afternoon injection (12-1 PM): sustains midday GH elevation. Evening injection (5-7 PM): final GH pulse but must occur 3-4 hours before bedtime to avoid interference with natural sleep-associated GH secretion. All injections require 30-60 minute fasting pre/post injection for optimal GH response.

FAQ: Hexarelin Dosage

What's the best starting dose for hexarelin?

50-100 mcg once daily for 3-5 days to assess tolerance. Most beginners progress to 100-150 mcg twice daily (200-300 mcg daily total) which produces excellent results.

Is higher dose always better?

No. Peak GH response occurs at moderate doses (150-200 mcg); doses above this produce diminishing returns while side effects increase proportionally. Receptor saturation occurs at approximately 200 mcg per injection.

Can beginners use three injections daily?

Not recommended for first cycle. Three daily injections (450-600 mcg total) is advanced protocol. Beginners should master twice-daily protocol first.

What if I don't respond well to standard doses?

Assess tolerance over 7-10 days, then increase doses incrementally by 25-50 mcg. If poor response persists at moderate doses (100-150 mcg), hexarelin may not be optimal choice; GHRP-6 or other secretagogues may work better. True non-responders are rare (<1%).
