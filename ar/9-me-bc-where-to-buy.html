ما هي شهادة التحليل ولماذا يهم؟

A Certificate of Analysis (COA) is a document provided by the buyer (or ideally, an independent third-party laboratory) certifying the identity, purity, and composition of a chemical batch. A legitimate COA includes: (1) Specific purity percentage of 9-Me-BC (should be 95%+ for research-grade quality); (2) Analysis method used (typically HPLC for purity determination); (3) Identification that the compound is indeed 9.Me-BC ( through mass spectrometry or NMR); (4)ing for common contaminants or heavy metals.
