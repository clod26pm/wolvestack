Frequently Asked Questions

How much fat can I lose on sermorelin?

5-12 lbs over 12 weeks is typical (more in higher body fat individuals). Fat loss is largest when combined with caloric deficit and training. Sermorelin alone without caloric deficit shows minimal fat loss.

Does sermorelin burn fat while you sleep?

Yes, partially. GH stimulates lipolysis throughout the day/night cycle. Night-time injection aligns sermorelin's effect with natural GH peaks during sleep, maximizing 24-hour fat burning.

Can I lose fat without being in a caloric deficit?

Sermorelin enhances fat loss but cannot fully overcome caloric surplus. Deficit of 300-500 kcal/day is recommended; sermorelin amplifies fat loss within that deficit by 25-40%.

Should I do cardio while using sermorelin for fat loss?

Yes. 150-200 min moderate cardio weekly or 75-100 min intense HIIT synergizes with sermorelin's fat-loss effects. Strength training preserves muscle during cutting.

Does sermorelin preferentially burn visceral fat?

Yes. GH preferentially mobilizes visceral fat, making sermorelin effective for reducing belly fat and improving metabolic markers even with modest total fat loss.

Can women use sermorelin for fat loss?

Yes. Women show similar relative fat loss but slightly preferential reduction from hips/thighs/breasts. Protocols are identical to men's.
