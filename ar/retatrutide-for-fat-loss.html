ما هو Retatrutide وكيف يعمل للخسائر البدينة؟

GLP-1/GIP/Glucagon Triple Mechanism for Fat Loss

GLP-1 activation increases satiety signaling in the hypothalamus, reduces hunger-promoting ghrelin, improves glucose homeostasis, slows gastric emptying (increasing fullness), and promotes insulin secretion in response to meals.
