ما هي آلية (إكستر مكس00) للعمل؟

CJC-1295 functions as a synthetic growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) analog, targeting GHRH receptors on somatotroph cells within the anterior pauitary gland. This peptide stimulates the synthesis and secretion of growth hormone through a physiological pathway similar to endogenous GHRH signaling. The four amino acid substitutions in CJC-1295 compared to native GHRH (positions 2, 8, 15, and 27) significantly enhance metabolic stability while maintaining full biological activity at the receptor level.
