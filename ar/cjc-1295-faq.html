ما هو CJC-1295؟

CJC-1295 (Modified GRF 1-29) is a synthetic Growth Hormone-Releasing Hormone (GHRH) analog-a peptide hormone that stimulates the anterior potuitary gland to secrete natural growth hormone. It contains the first 29 amino acids of natural GHRH plus four amino acid substitutions for enhanced stability.
