What Is ARA-290 and Why Does Dosing Matter?

ARA-290 (Erythropoietin-derived tissue-protective peptide) is a Innate repair receptor agonist, anti-inflammatory peptide. Synthetic 11-amino acid peptide engineered from the tissue-protective domain of erythropoietin (EPO). Getting the dosage right is critical — too little may produce no measurable effect, while excessive amounts increase risk without proportional benefit.
