Livagen side effects are minimal and mostly mild: headache (5-10%, detox symptom, resolves 3-5 days), nausea (2-5%, usually week 1 only), fatigue (5%, temporary). No serious adverse events reported. Incidence is much lower than many supplements. Side effects are typically detoxification responses indicating liver activation, not toxicity. Most users (85-90%) report zero side effects. Starting low dose (5mg weekly) minimizes risk.
