Dihexa demonstrates favorable safety in research: no serious adverse events in animal studies, minimal toxicity at supratherapeutic doses, and no organ damage or genetic toxicity. The theoretical concern is HGF/c-Met pathway involvement in tumor biology, but long-term animal studies showed no tumors.
