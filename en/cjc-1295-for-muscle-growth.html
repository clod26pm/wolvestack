Is CJC-1295 or testosterone better for muscle?

Testosterone typically builds more muscle per unit time. CJC-1295 superior for joint health, fat loss during bulking, and long-term sustainability. Different purposes.

Can beginners use CJC-1295 for muscle?

Yes, but unnecessary. Beginners gain 20-30 lbs in first year naturally; peptides offer diminishing returns. Consider at year 2+ of serious training.

What's the best training split with CJC-1295?

Upper/lower or push/pull/legs works equally well. Emphasis on compound movements. Higher volume capacity (more sets/reps) due to improved recovery.

Should I eat more on CJC-1295?

Yes; increased appetite + improved nutrient partitioning = surplus beneficial. Aim 300-500 kcal above maintenance during bulking phase.

Opposite; GH typically improves joint health. Some users report minor water retention initially, not pain. Usually resolves week 2-3.
