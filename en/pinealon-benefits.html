What Is Pinealon?

Pinealon (Glu-Asp-Arg tripeptide (EDR)) is a Tripeptide geroprotector, neuropeptide. Synthetic tripeptide derived from pineal gland secretions; Russian-developed geroprotector.

Pineal-selective tripeptide with demonstrated neuroprotection against both aging and trauma — one of the few peptides targeting the pineal gland specifically. It has attracted significant research interest for its potential effects on cognitive function improvement, neuroprotection, enhanced cerebellum protection, reduced brain cell apoptosis, emotional balance.

How Does Pinealon Produce These Benefits?

Activates gene expression and protein synthesis in neural tissues while reducing apoptosis. Displays neuroprotection through reduced oxidative stress, enhanced cerebellum neuron resistance, and improved mitochondrial function in aging brains.

This multi-pathway activity is why Pinealon shows potential across several different applications rather than being limited to a single use case.
