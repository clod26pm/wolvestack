Frequently Asked Questions

Which is stronger: hexarelin or GHRP-6?

Hexarelin is 3-5x more potent. Single hexarelin injection > multiple GHRP-6 doses. Hexarelin = advanced users; GHRP-6 = beginners.

Which causes more side effects?

Hexarelin. Stronger appetite, more prolactin/cortisol elevation, more water retention. GHRP-6 is gentler and better-tolerated overall.

Can I stack hexarelin + GHRP-6?

Not typically recommended. Redundant (both GH secretagogues). Better to stack either with CJC-1295 (GHRH) for synergy.

Which builds more muscle?

Hexarelin shows slightly faster gains (3-5% more) due to higher GH and prolactin elevation. Difference marginal; training/nutrition matters more.

Is hexarelin safer than GHRP-6?

No. GHRP-6 is safer—fewer side effects, lower cortisol/prolactin elevation, better tolerated long-term. Hexarelin is more aggressive.

For a beginner, which should I choose?

GHRP-6. Milder, more predictable, easier to manage. Master GHRP-6 before upgrading to hexarelin if desired.
