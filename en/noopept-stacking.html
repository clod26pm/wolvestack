Noopept stacks synergistically with choline (Alpha-GPC, CDP-choline), racetams (piracetam, aniracetam), L-theanine, and caffeine. The classic stack pairs Noopept + Choline + Piracetam for enhanced memory formation. Add caffeine/L-theanine for focus. Stack timing: Noopept first (T+0), then choline (T+5), racetams (T+10).
