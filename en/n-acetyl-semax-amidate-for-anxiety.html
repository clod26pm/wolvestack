Subcutaneous injection is preferred method using 31-gauge insulin syringes. Rotate injection sites (abdomen, arms, thighs) to prevent tissue buildup. Intranasal administration avoids needles but requires higher doses for equivalent effect. Both methods work effectively; choose based on personal preference and comfort level with injections.
