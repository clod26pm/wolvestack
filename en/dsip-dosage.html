DSIP Dosage Guide

Recommended Protocol & Cycle Length

📖 8 min read 🔬 Research & Education ⚠️ Not medical advice ✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Medical Disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. Not FDA-approved for human use. Consult a licensed healthcare professional. See full disclaimer.

Standard DSIP dosage is 100-500 mcg daily, typically administered in evening before bed. Effective dose range is 100-300 mcg for most users. Dose-dependent response: lower doses improve sleep quality; higher doses intensify dream effects. Start low (100 mcg), titrate upward as needed.
