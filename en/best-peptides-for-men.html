CJC-1295 and Ipamorelin for Growth Hormone

CJC-1295 (100-200 mcg 2-3x weekly) stimulates growth hormone-releasing hormone (GHRH) pathways. Ipamorelin (200-300 mcg daily or pre-sleep) stimulates ghrelin receptors, amplifying GH pulses. Combined, they increase endogenous growth hormone 2-3x baseline, producing lean mass gains, recovery enhancement, and metabolic improvements.

Effects: Increased muscle protein synthesis, faster recovery from training, improved body composition, enhanced sleep quality, and stronger sexual function. Results visible within 2-4 weeks; maximum benefits at 8-12 weeks.

Safety: No testicular suppression (preserves LH/FSH); no liver stress; no serious adverse effects. Cycle protocols (e.g., 12 weeks on, 4 weeks off) prevent receptor desensitization while maintaining benefits.

PT-141 for Erectile Function and Desire

PT-141 (Bremelanotide) directly activates melanocortin receptors in brain centers controlling sexual arousal and erectile function. It increases desire, improves erectile quality, and enhances sexual satisfaction. Clinical trials show effectiveness in erectile dysfunction and reduced sexual desire, even with normal testosterone.

Mechanism differs from sildenafil: PT-141 acts centrally (brain) rather than peripherally (penile blood vessels). It works with psychological factors and cardiovascular health, not against them. Effective with or without other sexual function peptides.

Dosing: 0.5-1 mg subcutaneous 30-45 minutes pre-sexual activity. Onset: 15-30 minutes. Effects: enhanced desire, improved erectile quality, increased satisfaction. Can be used on-demand or 2-3x weekly for sustained benefits.

BPC-157 for Penile Health and Recovery

BPC-157 increases blood flow to penile tissue, enhances nerve function, and supports smooth muscle relaxation—all critical for erectile function. It also supports systemic recovery (reduced inflammation, faster healing), benefiting overall sexual performance and endurance.

Dosing: 250-500 mcg subcutaneous or intranasal daily. Benefits erectile function, sexual endurance, and overall recovery. Synergizes with PT-141 and CJC/Ipamorelin.

Gonadorelin for Testosterone Optimization

Gonadorelin (GnRH analog) stimulates pituitary LH release, increasing testicular testosterone production. It maintains natural testosterone feedback loops, preserving testicular function and fertility—advantages over exogenous testosterone replacement.

Dosing: 100-200 mcg subcutaneous daily or 3x weekly. Results: increased testosterone within 1-2 weeks; improved sexual function, libido, and lean mass. Effects plateau at 4-8 weeks; pulsatile dosing maintains long-term efficacy.

Safe with indefinite use; preserves testicular size and function. Particularly valuable for individuals seeking testosterone optimization while maintaining fertility.

Kisspeptin for Testosterone Elevation

Kisspeptin stimulates GnRH secretion, increasing LH and testosterone through natural pathways. Research shows robust testosterone elevation, improved sexual function, and preserved fertility. Similar to Gonadorelin but may have additive effects when combined.

Dosing: 500 mcg-1 mg subcutaneous daily or 3x weekly. Results within 1-2 weeks; maximum effects at 4-8 weeks. Often combined with Gonadorelin for enhanced testosterone elevation.
