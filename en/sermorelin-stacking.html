Optimal stack: sermorelin 500-700 mcg + ipamorelin 200-300 mcg daily (synergistic GH elevation without excessive effects). Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best peptide to stack with sermorelin?

Ipamorelin is optimal; it's a GHRP secretagogue that amplifies sermorelin's GH signal without competing for receptors. 500 mcg sermorelin + 200 mcg ipamorelin is a popular stack.

Can I stack sermorelin with CJC-1295?

Cautiously. Both are GHRH agonists; stacking them can be excessive and increase side effect risk. CJC-1295's long half-life may suppress sermorelin's acute GH pulse.

Does stacking increase results proportionally?

No. Sermorelin + ipamorelin produces ~30-50% larger results than sermorelin alone, not 100% larger. Diminishing returns apply to peptide stacking.

Should I reduce sermorelin dose when stacking?

Yes. When stacking with ipamorelin or GHRP-6, reduce sermorelin by 20-30% to maintain optimal GH elevation without excessive side effects.

Can I stack sermorelin with testosterone?

Yes. Testosterone and sermorelin amplify each other. Monitor for excess water retention and estrogen-related effects. Lipid panels and hematocrit monitoring is recommended.

What's the most effective sermorelin stack?

Sermorelin (500-700 mcg) + ipamorelin (200-300 mcg) daily is considered the gold standard for body composition improvement with minimal excess side effects.
