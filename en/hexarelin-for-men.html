FAQ: For Men

Should I stack hexarelin with testosterone?

Not for first cycle. Master hexarelin monotherapy first. Stacking with testosterone is advanced protocol requiring experience with both compounds and understanding of combination synergies and risks.

Will hexarelin suppress my testosterone?

No direct suppression. However, elevated GH/IGF-1 may suppress endogenous GnRH through feedback mechanisms if excessively elevated. Moderate dosing and proper cycling prevent this. Hexarelin alone doesn't suppress testosterone like exogenous androgens do.

Can hexarelin improve sexual performance?

Possibly. Improved cardiovascular function and confidence from physique changes often enhance sexual performance. Elevated cortisol/prolactin may transiently suppress libido in sensitive individuals. Most males report improved sexual function overall.

Is hexarelin better than testosterone for beginners?

Different compounds. Hexarelin focuses on GH elevation and muscle building. Testosterone directly increases androgenic effects and muscle protein synthesis. Hexarelin is safer first protocol due to more reversible side effects. Testosterone is more potent but carries greater risks.
