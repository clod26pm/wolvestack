Lyophilized Dihexa should be stored at 2-8°C in darkness, remaining stable for 2-3 years. Reconstituted solution stays potent for 1-2 weeks refrigerated with bacteriostatic saline, or 3-5 days in sterile normal saline. Protect from light (store in amber vials or paper wrap), heat, and moisture.
