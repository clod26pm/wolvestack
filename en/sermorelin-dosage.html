Standard sermorelin dosage is 500-1000 mcg (0.5-1.0 mg) daily via subcutaneous injection, administered in evening before bed to align with natural GH secretion. Weight-based dosing: 10-15 mcg/kg for conservative protocols, 15-25 mcg/kg for aggressive protocols. Age adjustments: younger (30-40) use lower end, older (45-60) use higher end. Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical sermorelin dosage?

500-1000 mcg (0.5-1.0 mg) daily is standard. Weight-adjusted: 10-15 mcg/kg is conservative, 15-25 mcg/kg is aggressive. Age and goals modify this range.

Should I inject sermorelin in the morning or night?

Evening injection (before bed) aligns with natural GH secretion peaks during sleep. Morning injection is suboptimal but viable if evening administration is impossible.

Can I inject sermorelin every other day?

Yes. Alternate-day dosing reduces cumulative peptide exposure and may extend the response window. Results are typically 20-30% smaller than daily dosing.

What happens if I take too much sermorelin?

Excessive dosing increases side effects (arthralgia, carpal tunnel, water retention) without proportional benefit increase. Optimal therapeutic window is 500-1500 mcg/day.

How do I adjust my dose based on results?

After 4-6 weeks, if sleep/energy improve but body composition changes are minimal, increase dose by 100-200 mcg. If side effects emerge, reduce dose by 100-200 mcg.

Does sermorelin dosage need adjustment if I stack other peptides?

Yes. Stacking with CJC-1295 or GHRP-6 amplifies GH response; reduce sermorelin dose by 20-30% to maintain target GH elevation and avoid excessive effects.
