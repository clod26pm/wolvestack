Selank cycles typically run 4-8 weeks followed by 2-4 week breaks. Unlike benzodiazepines, selank does not create tolerance or dependence; continuous use is safe. Many practitioners cycle for psychological reset rather than pharmacological necessity. Benefits persist for 1-2 weeks post-cycle.
