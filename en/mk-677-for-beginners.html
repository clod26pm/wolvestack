Beginner Dosing Protocol

Week 1-2: 10 mg daily in evening on empty stomach. Assess appetite increase, lethargy, sleep quality, strength feeling. Week 3+: Increase to 15 mg based on tolerance. This dosage carries minimal side effects while showing excellent results. Most beginners never need to exceed 15 mg.

What to Expect Week-by-Week

Days 1-3: Sleep improves noticeably, appetite increases, mood improves. Week 1-2: Water retention (1-3 lbs), training feels better, recovery seems faster, strength improves slightly. Week 3-4: Appetite extremely high, visible muscle fullness, first strength gains 5-10%. Week 4-8: Visible muscle gain 2-5 lbs, strength gains 10-20%, body composition visibly improves. Week 8-12: Total gain 5-12 lbs lean mass, strength increases 15-25%, body looks significantly more muscular.

Training and Diet for Beginners

Training: 4-5 days/week progressive resistance training (gradually increase weight/reps). Proper form and progression critical. Diet: 300-500 calories above maintenance, 1.2-1.6 g protein per lb, consistent carbs/fats. Proper diet is NON-NEGOTIABLE; MK-677 amplifies good diet and bad diet equally.

First Cycle Duration

8 weeks: Minimum; results visible but not optimal. 10-12 weeks: Ideal for beginners; sufficient time for full benefits. 14-16 weeks: Longer but not necessary first cycle. Most beginners achieve excellent results in 12 weeks; extend only if maintaining good results and tolerating compound well.

Common Beginner Mistakes to Avoid

Expecting overnight results: Results visible week 4-6; be patient. Poor diet: Eating garbage despite MK-677; you won't gain muscle. Inconsistent training: Skipping workouts; training intensity is critical. Stacking too early: Adding testosterone, SARMs, or other compounds first cycle; master MK-677 solo first. Insufficient sleep: Undermines GH secretion; prioritize 8+ hours nightly.
