Hexarelin vs GHRP-6

Q: What's the difference? A: Hexarelin produces 4-6 fold greater GH elevation, more dramatic appetite stimulation, faster results (week 4-6 vs week 6-8), and approximately 20-30% greater total gains. Side effects (appetite, cortisol, prolactin) are proportionally greater. Cost is 1.5x GHRP-6. Choice depends on tolerance for side effects vs desired GH elevation magnitude.

Q: Which is better? A: Hexarelin for results-focused users with good side-effect tolerance. GHRP-6 for budget-conscious users or those with poor side-effect tolerance. Neither is objectively 'better'; choice depends on individual priorities.

Mechanics and Pharmacology

Q: How does hexarelin work? A: Binds ghrelin receptors (GHS-R1a) on pituitary cells, triggering GH secretion while suppressing somatostatin. Dual mechanism creates synergistic GH elevation superior to single-pathway compounds.

Q: What's the half-life? A: Distribution 8-12 minutes, elimination 2-3 hours. Requires 2-3 daily injections to maintain consistent GH elevation. Becomes undetectable in urine ~23 hours post-injection.

Usage and Administration

Q: How many injections daily? A: 2-3 depending on dose and cycle phase. Beginners typically 1 daily, progressing to 2 daily, advanced users 3 daily.

Q: Can I inject intramuscularly? A: Not recommended. Subcutaneous is the standard. Intramuscular causes more trauma and discomfort without superior results.

Results and Timeline

Q: When will I see results? A: Appetite/sleep changes within week 1-2. Strength gains by week 3-4. Visible muscle gain by week 6-8. Peak results week 8-10.

Q: How much muscle gain? A: 8-15 lbs per 12-week cycle with proper training/nutrition. Results vary 2-3 fold based on training quality, nutrition, sleep.

Side Effects and Safety

Q: Is hexarelin safe? A: Preliminary safety profile in research; long-term human safety data limited. Cycling (8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off) is conservative approach. No serious adverse events documented in research.

Q: What side effects should I expect? A: Appetite stimulation (most challenging), cortisol/prolactin elevation (transient), water retention (2-5 lbs), sleep disruption if dosed late evening. All resolve 4-8 weeks post-cycle.

Cycling and Tolerance

Q: How long should I cycle? A: 8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off minimum. Longer cycles (16+ weeks) not recommended without medical supervision. Off-cycle allows receptor resensitization.

Q: Will I develop tolerance? A: Yes, after 3-4 weeks continuous use, GH response declines 20-40%. Tolerance is prevented through appropriate cycling. Cycling breaks are essential.

Stacking

Q: Can I stack hexarelin with other compounds? A: Yes, synergistic with CJC-1295 (producing 10-15 fold GH elevation). Additive with ipamorelin (same pathway). Synergistic with testosterone but requires monitoring.

Cost and Value

Q: How much does hexarelin cost? A: Approximately $50-80 per 100 mcg vial; 1.5x GHRP-6 cost. A 12-week cycle costs $400-800 depending on dosing and vendor.

Q: Is hexarelin worth the cost vs GHRP-6? A: For users achieving good tolerance and results, yes. For budget-conscious, GHRP-6 at half cost may be preferable despite 20-30% less dramatic results.
