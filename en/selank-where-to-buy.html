What Is Selank and Why Source Matters?

Selank is a research peptide: seven amino acids in specific sequence. Impure or misidentified Selank wastes money and poses contamination risks if injected. Sourcing from verified vendors with third-party testing is not optional—it's essential for safety and efficacy.

Criteria for Choosing a Selank Vendor

1) Third-party COA testing (independent lab verification of purity/identity). 2) Published COA available for inspection. 3) Transparent company information (physical address, clear contact methods). 4) Consistent product quality (batch-to-batch testing). 5) Professional website (no obvious red flags). 6) Realistic pricing ($40-80 per vial typical). 7) Customer reviews from reputable communities.

What Does a Certificate of Analysis Tell You?

COA should show: (1) Peptide name and sequence, (2) HPLC purity percentage (should be >95%), (3) Molecular weight verification, (4) Testing date and laboratory name, (5) Batch number matching your product. If any of these are missing, request it. A legitimate vendor can provide this.

Red Flags: Avoiding Scams

Avoid vendors if: (1) No COA available or 'lost.' (2) Claiming 100% purity (unrealistic; 95-98% is realistic maximum). (3) Suspiciously cheap pricing (<$25/vial = likely impure or misidentified). (4) No company information or physical address. (5) Only cash/cryptocurrency payment (harder to reverse fraud). (6) Vague or defensive about sourcing. (7) Promising therapeutic claims (it's 'research only').

Powder vs Liquid Selank

Powder: more cost-effective per dose, easier to verify quality via COA testing, requires reconstitution. Liquid (pre-reconstituted): convenient but costs 2-3x more, harder to verify concentration independently, shorter shelf life. For cost-conscious buyers: powder. For convenience: liquid.

Pricing Analysis

30-mg Selank vial typical pricing: $40-80 depending on vendor and purity. International shipping may add $10-30. Bulk discounts common (10% off at 3+ vials). Suspiciously cheap (<$25) or expensive (>$100) vendors warrant skepticism. Mid-range ($50-65) vendors typically offer best quality/price balance.

Shipping and Import Considerations

Selank is not a controlled substance in most countries; legal to ship within many regions. Domestic shipping: 2-5 days, no custom risk. International shipping: 5-14 days, possible customs inspection (usually passes; no controlled substance status). Some vendors ship internationally; others domestic-only. Clarify before purchase.

Reconstitution and Storage After Purchase

Once received, store powder at 2-8°C (refrigerator) immediately. Use bacteriostatic water for reconstitution (1-2 mg/mL typical concentration). Reconstituted liquid requires freezer storage (-18°C). Proper storage preserves quality; poor storage degrades peptide regardless of initial quality.
