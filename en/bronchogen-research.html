What Is the Evidence for Bronchogen's Mechanism?

Operates through gene expression modulation — interacts with DNA regulatory sequences and histone proteins in lung cells to reduce pro-inflammatory cytokines, promote regeneration of bronchial epithelium, and restore ciliated cell populations. Also enhances local mucosal immunity by increasing secretory IgA.

These pathways have been identified through in vitro studies, animal models, and where available, human trials.
