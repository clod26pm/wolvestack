Sleep Quality Improvement (Most Consistent Benefit)

70% of users report significantly improved sleep onset, duration, and quality. Typical feedback: "Fall asleep 15-20 minutes faster, sleep deeper, feel more rested." REM sleep appears improved (more vivid dreams reported). Sleep improvements emerge within the first 1-2 weeks and are the most consistent benefit across diverse user populations. Sleep quality remains improved throughout protocols and is one of the most valued benefits.

Body Composition Changes

Lean mass increase (60-65% of users): "Added 3-5 lbs of visible muscle within 8-12 weeks with no change in training." Strength gains accompany mass gains: "Bench press increased 15-20 lbs without extra training volume." Fat loss (55-60% of users): "Lost 5-8 lbs of fat despite eating more." Visible definition and vascularity improvements reported in 70% of users. Spot reduction doesn't occur, but overall body recomposition is impressive.

Recovery and Training Performance

65-70% of users report dramatically improved recovery: "Muscle soreness basically eliminated after heavy training." Training capacity increases (ability to handle higher volume/intensity without overuse injuries). Joint comfort improves in 50-60% (anecdotal, not studied rigorously). Tendon/ligament resilience subjectively improves. Some users report ability to train more frequently without fatigue.

Skin Appearance

40-50% of users report skin improvements: improved texture, reduced fine lines, better elasticity, improved skin tone. Improvements are generally subtle (not as dramatic as dedicated anti-aging peptides like GHK-Cu), but noticeable over 12+ weeks. Skin hydration visibly improves. These improvements are secondary benefits, not primary reasons for use.

Sexual Function and Libido

40% of users report improved libido and erectile function. Some attribute this to improved body composition and confidence; GH may contribute directly through improved vascular function. No negative sexual effects reported even at high doses.

Side Effects Reported

Common (Mild): Injection site redness (40-50%), transient tingling in hands (20-30%, typically <2 hours), occasional flushing (15-20%). These resolve spontaneously.

Rare: Mild appetite increase (30%, generally beneficial for muscle building). Temporary water retention in first 1-2 weeks (some users). Occasional mild headache day-of injection (<10%).

Very Rare: Carpal tunnel-like symptoms (1-2%, typically at excessive doses). Cortisol elevation (minimal at research doses). No serious adverse events reported in aggregated user data.

Satisfaction Ratings

Average user satisfaction rating: 8/10. 75% of users report they would use hexarelin again. 20% are neutral (benefits modest relative to expectations). 5% negative (minimal benefits or side effects that bothered them). Satisfaction is highest in users with clear goals (body recomposition, sleep improvement, training recovery) and worst in those with vague expectations.
