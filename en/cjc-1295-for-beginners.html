Frequently Asked Questions

What makes CJC-1295 good for beginners?

Beginner-friendly dose range (50-200 mcg), predictable effects, minimal side effects at low doses, works solo without stacking required.

Can I inject CJC-1295 myself?

Yes; subcutaneous injection into abdomen or thigh. Needle is very small (29-31 gauge), pain minimal. Self-injection is standard for all peptides.

How long until I see results?

Sleep and appetite improve days 1-7. Visible muscle/fat changes week 3-4. Peak results week 8-12. Patience required.

Do I need to cycle CJC-1295?

Recommended but not mandatory. Standard: 12-16 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off. Cycling preserves receptor sensitivity and reduces theoretical long-term risk.

What happens if I miss an injection?

No danger. If you miss one day, inject next scheduled day. If you miss an entire week, resume normal schedule. Continuity matters more than perfection.

Can I combine CJC-1295 with other peptides?

Not recommended for first cycle. Master CJC-1295 alone first (16+ weeks experience). Then add GHRP-6 or TB-500 if desired.
