Sermorelin (GHRH agonist) and MK-677 (ghrelin mimic/GHSR agonist) both stimulate growth hormone but via different mechanisms. Sermorelin: faster results (4-8 weeks), more potent GH elevation (4-5x baseline), shorter half-life (requires daily injection), better for acute goals (body composition, strength). MK-677: slower results (8-12 weeks), moderate GH elevation (2-3x baseline), long half-life (once-daily oral), sustained elevation, better for long-term anti-aging. Head-to-head: sermorelin produces faster/larger results; MK-677 is more convenient and requires less dose. Cost: MK-677 typically cheaper ($50-100/month vs $150-300/month for sermorelin).

Frequently Asked Questions

Which produces faster results: sermorelin or MK-677?

Sermorelin. Results visible by week 6-8 vs MK-677 at week 8-12. Sermorelin's acute GH spike is more anabolic; MK-677's sustained elevation is better for long-term adaptation.

Which is stronger for muscle gain?

Sermorelin. 4-6 lbs muscle gain over 12 weeks vs MK-677's 2-4 lbs. Sermorelin's more potent GH elevation and daily injection consistency favor muscle building.

Which has fewer side effects?

Similar side effect profiles. MK-677 may cause more appetite increase (ghrelin-driven); sermorelin causes more acute water retention. Overall comparable.

Which is cheaper?

MK-677 ($50-100/month) is cheaper than sermorelin ($150-300/month). Cost is a major factor for many users favoring MK-677.

Can I stack sermorelin and MK-677?

Yes, they synergize via different mechanisms. Sermorelin (GHRH) + MK-677 (ghrelin) amplify each other. Stack produces ~1.5x results of either alone but at increased cost and side effect risk.

Which should beginners use?

MK-677 is often recommended for beginners due to ease (oral, no injection), lower cost, and sufficient results. Sermorelin for those willing to inject and wanting faster results.
