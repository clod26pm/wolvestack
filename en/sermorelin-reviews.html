Frequently Asked Questions

What percentage of users see results?

~75-85% report noticeable positive results. Non-responders are typically non-compliant with training/nutrition/sleep or have very low baseline GH sensitivity.

What do users praise most about sermorelin?

Sleep improvement (most consistent), energy/recovery boost, visible strength gains, and body composition changes are the most-praised benefits.

What are common user complaints?

Results take time (impatience is main complaint), water retention (most common side effect complaint), cost, and initial injection anxiety/needle anxiety.

Are most user reviews positive?

Yes. 75-85% positive, 10-15% neutral (some results but not life-changing), 5-10% negative (non-responders or serious side effects). Overall sentiment is positive.

Do women report good results?

Yes, equally positive as men. Women often praise fat loss results and skin quality improvements specifically. Body composition changes are slightly smaller but meaningful.

How important is training/nutrition to results?

Critical. Users with poor training/nutrition report 50% of results seen in users with optimized training/nutrition. Sermorelin amplifies; it doesn't create results from nothing.
