GHK-Cu beginners guide: start with 0.5-1% concentration serum, apply once daily (evening), allow 2-4 weeks to assess tolerability, then increase to daily application if well-tolerated. Getting Started: Product Selection

Choose reputable skincare brand GHK-Cu serum (0.5-1% concentration recommended for beginners). Look for: clear product labeling, transparent ingredient list, minimal added fragrances (fragrance = irritation risk), and brands with good customer reviews. Price range: $20-60 per bottle (lasts 1-2 months depending on application amount).

Patch Test and Tolerance Assessment

Before full-face application: apply small amount (pea-size) to inner arm or behind ear, leave for 24 hours, check for redness/irritation. If no reaction, proceed to full-face application. Start with 2-3x weekly application (e.g., Monday/Wednesday/Friday), then increase to daily if well-tolerated. Most people tolerate GHK-Cu without issues, but patch testing is prudent.

Application Protocol for Beginners

Evening Routine: Gentle cleanser → pat dry → apply GHK-Cu serum (1-2 pumps to face/neck) → wait 1-2 minutes → light moisturizer → done.

Morning (Optional): Gentle cleanser → GHK-Cu serum (optional) → sunscreen SPF 30+ (essential when using anti-aging peptides).

Frequency: Start 3-4x weekly, increase to daily if tolerated. Once daily is sufficient; twice daily provides no additional benefit.

Realistic Expectations

Weeks 0-2: no obvious changes, skin may feel slightly different. Weeks 2-4: subtle texture improvement, skin slightly firmer-feeling. Weeks 4-8: obvious fine line reduction, improved firmness, visible in photos. Weeks 8-12: significant improvements, collagen remodeling evident. Don't expect dramatic transformation; expect moderate, gradual improvements.

Common Beginner Mistakes

Mistake 1: Expecting overnight results (peptides take weeks). Mistake 2: Overusing (once daily is sufficient). Mistake 3: Skipping sunscreen (essential with peptides). Mistake 4: Combining too many actives at once (start simple, add later). Mistake 5: Discontinuing too early (need 8-12 weeks minimum to judge efficacy).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GHK-Cu suitable for beginners?

Yes—it's gentle and effective. Good entry point to peptide skincare. More forgiving than retinoids or vitamin C for new users.

Can I combine GHK-Cu with other skincare immediately?

Start simple: cleanser, GHK-Cu, moisturizer, sunscreen. After 4 weeks, add other products if desired. Avoid combining with multiple actives immediately (irritation risk).

What if GHK-Cu irritates my skin?

Rare. If irritation occurs: reduce frequency (3x weekly instead of daily), reduce concentration (0.5% instead of 1%), or discontinue. Patch test first prevents surprises.

When can I see results?

Week 4-8 typically shows obvious improvements in photos. Week 12 shows significant results. Patience is essential—peptides aren't fast-acting.

Should I combine GHK-Cu with retinoids?

Later (after 4 weeks of GHK-Cu alone). Once skin tolerates GHK-Cu, adding retinoids (2-3x weekly, separate nights) creates powerful anti-aging combination.
