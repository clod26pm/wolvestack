Selank Injection Guide

Research-Supported Evidence & Protocols

Medical Disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. Not FDA-approved for human use. Consult a licensed healthcare professional. See full disclaimer.

Selank injection: 27-29 gauge needle, 0.3-1.0 mL volume, subcutaneous (belly, thigh, arm), alternate sites daily. Intranasal: 250-500 mcg in 50-100 uL volume, one nostril per dose to avoid overflow. Injection site reactions rare; intranasal occasionally causes mild nasal irritation that resolves within minutes.
