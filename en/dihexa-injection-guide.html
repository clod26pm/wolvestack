Dihexa is administered via subcutaneous injection using 29-31 gauge insulin syringes, typically 50-100 mcg daily or every other day. Rotate injection sites (abdomen, thighs, arms) to prevent lipodystrophy. Proper sterile technique, slow injection speed (3-5 seconds), and correct needle angle ensure painless administration.
