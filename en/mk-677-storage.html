MK-677 requires cool, dry storage (2-8°C refrigeration is ideal) to maintain stability. Once reconstituted with bacteriostatic water, the solution remains stable for 20-30 days refrigerated. Proper storage prevents degradation and ensures consistent potency across the cycle.

How Should Raw MK-677 Powder Be Stored?

Raw MK-677 powder should be stored in airtight containers in a cool, dark place. Temperatures between 2-8°C (refrigerator) are optimal. Avoid freezing, which can degrade the compound. Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture. Most suppliers provide powder sealed in vacuum-packs; store unopened packs refrigerated until ready to reconstitute.

Refrigeration vs Room Temperature Storage

Refrigerated storage (2-8°C) extends stability and is recommended for long-term storage (>6 months). Room temperature storage (20-25°C) is acceptable for shorter periods (1-3 months) if kept in dark, airtight containers. Temperatures above 25°C accelerate degradation. Never expose MK-677 to heat, light, or humidity.

Reconstitution and Solution Stability

MK-677 is typically reconstituted with bacteriostatic water (sterile water with 0.9% benzyl alcohol preservative). Reconstituted solution is stable for 20-30 days when refrigerated (2-8°C). Some sources claim stability up to 60 days if kept sterile and refrigerated. Once opened, the reconstituted vial should be used or discarded after expiration.

Preventing Degradation and Contamination

Use sterile technique when reconstituting and drawing doses to prevent bacterial contamination. Avoid repeated freeze-thaw cycles, which degrade the peptide. Keep reconstituted solution in a dark glass vial (not clear plastic, which allows light penetration). Store away from direct sunlight. Never leave the vial at room temperature.

Signs of Degradation

Degraded MK-677 shows cloudiness in solution, discoloration (yellowing or browning), or particle formation. If reconstituted solution develops these signs, discard it. Degraded peptide may lose potency or potentially introduce contaminants. Always store properly to maintain integrity.

Long-Term Storage Beyond One Cycle

For storage beyond 1-2 months, freeze dried powder in airtight containers at 2-8°C or below. Avoid opening frequently. For reconstituted solution, store at 2-8°C and replace vials monthly if not using. Long-term storage (>6 months) requires freezer temperatures (-20°C or below) and airtight packaging.

Traveling with MK-677: Considerations

MK-677 powder is easiest to travel with (doesn't require refrigeration for short periods). Reconstituted solution requires ice packs and insulated transport. Check local laws before traveling with peptides internationally. Some countries prohibit peptide transport. Within the US, personal transport is generally legal but undeclared travel to other countries may violate import laws.

Quality Verification Before Reconstitution

Before reconstituting, inspect the powder for color, consistency, and packaging integrity. White to off-white powder is normal. Any discoloration, clumping, or moisture suggests prior storage issues. Verify the seal is unbroken. Purchase from reputable sources with documented storage practices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I freeze MK-677 powder?

Freezing is acceptable for powder (doesn't damage it) but unnecessary. Standard refrigeration (2-8°C) is sufficient. Avoid freeze-thaw cycles of reconstituted solution.

How long does reconstituted MK-677 last?

Reconstituted solution remains stable for 20-30 days refrigerated (2-8°C). Some claim up to 60 days with sterile technique and darkness. Discard after expiration or if cloudiness develops.

Should I store in a fridge or freezer?

Refrigerator (2-8°C) is ideal for powder and reconstituted solution. Freezing is unnecessary for powder and risks degrading reconstituted solution if freeze-thaw cycling occurs.

What is bacteriostatic water?

Bacteriostatic water is sterile water with 0.9% benzyl alcohol (preservative). This prevents bacterial growth in the reconstituted peptide solution. Use only bacteriostatic water for MK-677, not regular sterile water.

Can I store MK-677 in a regular cabinet?

Not recommended long-term. MK-677 powder can tolerate room temperature for 1-3 months in dark, airtight containers, but refrigeration is safer. Reconstituted solution must be refrigerated.

How do I know if my MK-677 is degraded?

Cloudiness, discoloration (yellowing, browning), or particle formation indicate degradation. Degraded peptide should be discarded. Purchase from reputable sources and store properly to avoid this.
