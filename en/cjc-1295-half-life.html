CJC-1295 Half-Life

Understanding Pharmacokinetics for Optimal Dosing

Medical Disclaimer

CJC-1295 half-life: 6-8 hours in circulation. GH peaks 2-4 hours post-injection, returns to baseline by 8-10 hours. This rapid kinetics requires 2-3 injections weekly for consistent GH elevation. Modified versions (DAC) extend half-life to 14+ days, requiring only weekly injection. Understanding half-life optimizes injection timing and dose frequency.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does CJC-1295 need multiple injections?

Short half-life (6-8 hrs) means single injection provides GH boost for only 8-10 hours. Multiple injections maintain consistent elevation across the day.

Is CJC-1295 DAC better than regular CJC-1295?

Different not better. DAC extends half-life (14+ days), requires weekly injection. Regular CJC requires 2-3x weekly. Both work; differs by convenience and preference.

Can I inject once weekly instead of twice?

You could, but effects are suboptimal. Single injection = single GH spike. Most protocols 2-3x weekly for sustained elevation.

Does kidney/liver function affect half-life?

Yes. Poor renal or hepatic function extends half-life. Less frequent dosing needed; higher risk of accumulation and side effects.

Should I adjust timing based on training?

Optional. Training doesn't dramatically affect half-life. Consistency matters more than precise timing. Same time daily is easiest.

How long until steady-state GH levels?

7-10 days of consistent dosing. Full steady-state effects (muscle gain, skin improvement) take 4-8 weeks due to downstream effects, not just GH levels.
