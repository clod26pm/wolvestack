Standard Orexin-A dosage is 10-20 mcg administered intranasally once daily, usually in the morning (7-9 AM) for wakefulness support. New users start at 10 mcg; adjust by 5 mcg increments based on response. Effects peak 30-60 minutes post-administration. Maximum daily dose: 30 mcg. Doses above 20 mcg rarely provide additional benefit.
