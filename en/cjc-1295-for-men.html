Frequently Asked Questions

Does CJC-1295 directly increase testosterone?

Indirectly. CJC-1295 boosts GH; elevated GH enhances testicular LH sensitivity and testosterone production. Effect is modest—10-30% increase—not replacement-level.

Can I combine with TRT?

Yes, and synergistically. CJC-1295 enhances TRT effects on sexual function and mood. Monitor testosterone levels; may need TRT dose adjustment.

Will this improve erectile dysfunction?

Possibly, if ED is GH-deficiency related. Won't resolve ED from vascular disease or severe hypogonadism alone. Best for mild ED + low energy.

What about prostate health?

CJC-1295 doesn't directly affect prostate cancer risk. Normal prostate screening recommended if using any androgens chronically. No specific prostate concerns.

How much sexual function improvement?

Variable; 50-80% report improved libido, sexual confidence, and erectile quality. Improvements compound over 8-12 weeks. Individual response highly variable.

Does this improve fertility?

CJC-1295 doesn't impair fertility like anabolic steroids. May marginally improve it via improved testosterone and energy. Not a fertility treatment.
