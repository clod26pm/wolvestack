Noopept works by increasing nerve growth factor (NGF) and brain-derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF), enhancing neuroplasticity and synaptic function. It also improves neuronal membrane fluidity, reduces neuroinflammation, and protects against oxidative stress. These combined mechanisms enhance memory formation, cognitive flexibility, and neuroprotection.
