Frequently Asked Questions

How much body fat can you lose on sermorelin?

Typical fat loss is 5-12 lbs over 12 weeks (more in individuals with higher starting body fat). This translates to 3-8% reduction in total body fat. Higher doses and optimized nutrition/training accelerate fat loss; poor diet negates fat loss entirely.

Can you build muscle without training on sermorelin?

Sermorelin amplifies the muscle-building response to training but does not create muscle gain without training stimulus. Training is essential for meaningful muscle gain.

Why are some before and after results fake?

Some before photos are taken at poor angles, poor lighting, and high body fat/bloat; after photos are taken with optimal lighting and angle. Realistic photos taken in identical conditions show more modest but genuine changes.

How quickly can you see results on sermorelin?

Sleep improvements appear by week 2-3. Energy improvements appear by week 3-4. Body composition changes become visible around week 6-8 with proper training and nutrition.

Can sermorelin results plateau?

Yes. Most rapid improvements occur weeks 1-12. By week 16-20, the rate of additional improvement slows significantly. Cycling can reset adaptation.

Do women and men see different before and after results?

Women show similar relative improvements but slightly different composition: women may see more preferential fat loss from hips/thighs; men see more muscle gain in shoulders/chest.
