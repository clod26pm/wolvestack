Frequently Asked Questions

Is CJC-1295 + GHRP-6 better than CJC-1295 alone?

Yes. GHRP-6 stimulates GH secretion; CJC-1295 enhances GH-releasing hormone. Together: synergistic, 2-3x greater GH elevation. Most popular stack.

How often should I inject the stack?

Typically 2-3x weekly, same injection time. Can inject CJC-1295 + GHRP-6 simultaneously from same syringe or separate syringes. Frequency unchanged.

What about CJC-1295 + ipamorelin?

Also synergistic but slightly gentler than GHRP-6. Fewer side effects, slightly lower GH peak. Good for those sensitive to GHRP-6 side effects.

Can I add three peptides at once?

Possible but rarely recommended for beginners. CJC-1295 + GHRP-6 + TB-500 is sensible (healing focus). Adding semaglutide complicates management.

Does stacking increase side effects?

Slightly. More peptides = more GH = higher risk of headache, appetite increase, water retention. Still well-tolerated if doses stay therapeutic.

Is stacking cost-effective?

Depends on goals. For muscle + healing: yes, stacking outperforms solo use. For anti-aging: no, CJC-1295 alone sufficient.
