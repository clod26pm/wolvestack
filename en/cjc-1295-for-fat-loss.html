Frequently Asked Questions

Does CJC-1295 suppress appetite?

Indirectly, via improved satiety hormones. Not as potent as semaglutide for appetite suppression, but noticeable reduction in hunger on most protocols.

How much fat can I expect to lose?

5-12 lbs per 12-week cycle in caloric deficit. Losses higher with aggressive deficit/training; lower with minimal deficit. Expect 30-50% more fat loss than diet alone.

Can I use CJC-1295 year-round for fat loss?

Not recommended. 12-16 week cycles on, 4-8 weeks off is standard. Continuous use reduces receptor sensitivity and increases side effect risk.

Should I combine with semaglutide?

Yes, if budget allows. CJC-1295 preserves muscle; semaglutide suppresses appetite. Synergistic for aggressive cutting. Requires careful monitoring.

Do I need to eat more on CJC-1295?

Paradoxically no. Increased metabolism can partially offset increased appetite. Total calories still determine fat loss; increased food intake doesn't prevent loss.

What cardio should I do with CJC-1295?

Standard cardio (20-40 min, 3-4x weekly) optimal. No need to increase dramatically. CJC-1295 itself doesn't require special cardio protocols.
