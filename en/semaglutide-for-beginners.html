Semaglutide for weight loss starts with 0.25mg weekly injections, escalating by 0.25mg every 4 weeks to 2.4mg. Expect appetite suppression, nausea, and GI changes in weeks 1-8 that usually resolve by week 12. Weight loss begins by week 4, with 5-10 lbs typical by month 3. Beginners should prepare for appetite suppression (eat less but nutritious meals), manage side effects (small frequent meals, ginger), and prioritize high protein intake.

How Do You Start Semaglutide as a Beginner?

Step 1: Consult healthcare provider; confirm suitability (BMI >27 with comorbidity or >30). Step 2: Receive prescription for Wegovy (weight loss) or Ozempic off-label. Step 3: Learn injection technique (subcutaneous, abdomen/thigh/upper arm). Step 4: Set expectations—results take 8-12 weeks; side effects are temporary.

What Should Beginners Prepare Before Starting Semaglutide?

Stock anti-nausea aids (ginger candies, peppermint tea, motion sickness bands). Buy smaller plates and bowls to adjust to reduced portion sizes. Prepare high-protein foods (chicken, fish, eggs, yogurt). Download a food tracking app. Schedule weight-in checkpoints (weekly or bi-weekly). Arrange provider follow-ups every 4 weeks.

What Happens in the First Week on Semaglutide?

Week 1-2: minimal side effects at 0.25mg; appetite suppression may be subtle. Some nausea or food aversion possible. No significant weight loss yet. Key: establish injection routine, begin dietary adjustment, start side effect monitoring.

What Happens by Week 4 on Semaglutide?

Week 4: appetite suppression becomes pronounced; nausea peaks (improves by week 6); first weight loss appears (2-5 lbs typical). Food portions automatically reduce; eating >50% of normal meal feels uncomfortable. GI side effects (constipation or mild diarrhea) common.

What Should Beginners Eat on Semaglutide?

Focus on nutrient density over volume: lean proteins (chicken, fish, tofu), whole grains, vegetables, fruits. Avoid high-fat, sugary foods (cause nausea and GI distress). Eat 3 meals + 1-2 snacks daily (smaller portions than pre-semaglutide). Example: 4oz chicken + 1 cup broccoli + ½ cup rice per meal.

How Much Food Can You Eat on Semaglutide?

Typical portions drop 50-70%. Pre-treatment: 8oz chicken + 2 cups rice = comfortable. On semaglutide: 4oz chicken + ½ cup rice = full feeling. This suppression is automatic—not willful restriction. Forcing larger meals risks nausea/vomiting.

When Should Beginners Expect Significant Weight Loss?

Week 1-4: 2-5 lbs (mostly water). Week 5-8: 5-10 lbs (fat + water). Week 9-16: 10-20 lbs. By month 6: 20-40 lbs typical at 2.4mg. Vary greatly by individual; some lose faster, others slower.

How Should Beginners Handle Side Effects?

Nausea: small frequent meals, ginger, peppermint tea, motion sickness bands. Vomiting: anti-nausea medication (ondansetron if severe). Diarrhea: soluble fiber, loperamide, hydration. Constipation: water, fiber, movement. Most resolve by week 8-12 without intervention.

Should Beginners Adjust Exercise on Semaglutide?

Weeks 1-8: light exercise only (walking). Week 9+: gradually resume normal exercise as nausea resolves and energy improves. Avoid intense exercise during acute nausea phase—increases GI distress. Hydration is critical throughout.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you inject semaglutide?

Subcutaneous injection (under skin) into abdomen, thigh, or upper arm. Once weekly on the same day. Pinch skin, insert needle at 90-degree angle, inject 1 second, withdraw. Rotate injection sites to prevent lipohypertrophy.

What if you forget to inject semaglutide?

If <2 days late, inject immediately. If >2 days late, skip the dose; resume the following week on original day. Never double-dose.

Is nausea normal in the first week?

Mild nausea is common; severe vomiting is not. Mild nausea usually resolves by week 6. Severe vomiting warrants provider consultation.

Can beginners go off semaglutide if side effects are bad?

Yes. If side effects are intolerable by week 4, discuss with provider. Options: slow dose escalation, temporary discontinuation, or alternative GLP-1 agonist. Don't abruptly stop without guidance.

Should beginners diet on semaglutide?

Formal dieting is unnecessary—appetite suppression creates automatic restriction. Focus on eating nutritious, protein-rich foods in smaller portions. Avoid willful under-eating below 1,200 kcal/day.

How long before beginners see noticeable weight loss?

Most see changes by week 4 (clothes fit differently, pants loosen). Visible body composition changes (face shape, waist definition) appear by week 8-12.
