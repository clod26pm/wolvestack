Affiliate Disclosure

FTC-compliant transparency about how WolveStack earns revenue

What This Means

WolveStack participates in affiliate programs with select peptide vendors. When you click a vendor link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Which Links Are Affiliate Links

Vendor links in our sourcing guide and individual article sidebar vendor cards may be affiliate links. We label these where possible.

How This Affects Our Reviews

It doesn't. Our vendor rankings are based on purity testing data, third-party COA documentation, community reputation, and customer experience — not commission rates. We have turned down higher-commission vendors who didn't meet our quality bar.

Vendors We Work With

We only affiliate with vendors we have independently assessed as meeting minimum quality standards: HPLC-verified purity, third-party testing, transparent sourcing.
