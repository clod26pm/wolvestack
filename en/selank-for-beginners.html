Selank for beginners: Start with 250 mcg once daily, either subcutaneous injection or intranasal administration. Conservative approach assesses individual response without overwhelming effects. First 3 days: subtle mood lift. Week 1: pronounced anxiety reduction. Weeks 2-4: mood stabilization and cognitive benefits. Beginner-friendly with minimal side effects.
