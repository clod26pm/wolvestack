Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between sermorelin and HGH?

Sermorelin stimulates your own GH production; HGH is exogenous hormone. Sermorelin is gentler, more natural, but produces smaller effects. HGH is stronger but carries higher shutdown risk.

How long before seeing sermorelin benefits?

Sleep and energy improve by week 2-3. Strength increases appear by week 3-4. Body composition changes become visible week 6-8. Skin/hair improvements show by week 8-12.

What peptides stack well with sermorelin?

CJC-1295, ipamorelin, and GHRP-6 stack synergistically with sermorelin to amplify GH response. Avoid multiple GHRH agonists simultaneously.

Can women use sermorelin for benefits?

Yes. Women benefit from sermorelin equally; results are slightly smaller due to lower baseline androgens amplifying GH effects less in women. Fat loss often occurs preferentially from hips/thighs.

Is sermorelin safe for long-term use?

Research suggests sermorelin is well-tolerated. Long-term human safety data (24+ months) is limited. Cycling (8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off) is recommended to avoid tolerance.

How do I know sermorelin is working?

Improved sleep (first sign), increased energy (week 3-4), visible strength gains (week 4-6), and body composition changes (week 8+) are reliable indicators of response.
