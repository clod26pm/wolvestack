Complete First-Time User Protocol

Beginner sermorelin protocol: start with 500-600 mcg daily for weeks 1-2 to assess tolerance, then increase to 700-900 mcg for weeks 3-10. Inject subcutaneously at night. Expect sleep improvement by week 2-3, energy boost by week 3-4, and body composition changes by week 8. Most important: maintain consistent training, adequate protein (1.6+ g/kg), and 7-9 hours sleep. Common beginner mistakes include skipping sleep, poor nutrition, inconsistent dosing, and unrealistic expectations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is sermorelin safe for my first time?

Yes. Sermorelin is well-tolerated and one of the gentler performance peptides. Start with conservative doses (500-600 mcg) to assess individual response.

How do I inject sermorelin for the first time?

Clean injection site with alcohol. Pinch skin and inject subcutaneously at 45-degree angle using 30-31 gauge needle. Rotate sites daily. Watch tutorial videos to build confidence.

What should I eat while on sermorelin?

Increase protein to 1.6-2.2 g/kg, maintain slight caloric surplus for muscle gain (if goals are hypertrophy) or caloric deficit for fat loss (if goals are cutting). Hydration is critical.

How quickly will I feel sermorelin effects?

Sleep improvement by week 2-3 (earliest sign), energy and recovery boost by week 3-4, visible strength gains by week 4-6, body composition changes by week 8-10.

Should I cycle off sermorelin or use it continuously?

Cycling (8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off) prevents adaptation and is recommended. Continuous use beyond 16 weeks shows diminishing returns.

What if I don't see results after 6 weeks?

Check: Are you sleeping 7-9 hours? Is your protein intake adequate (1.6+ g/kg)? Is your dose 700+ mcg? Is your training consistent? Most common issue is inadequate sleep or nutrition.
