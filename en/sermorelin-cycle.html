Frequently Asked Questions

How long should a sermorelin cycle be?

8-12 weeks active is optimal. Shorter cycles (<6 weeks) show suboptimal results; longer cycles (>16 weeks) increase adaptation risk. 10-week cycles are common.

Should I taper sermorelin at the end?

Yes. Gradual tapering (reduce dose 25-50% for final 2 weeks) allows HPTA recovery and minimizes rebound. Abrupt discontinuation is fine but less elegant.

Can I cycle sermorelin multiple times?

Yes. Rest periods (4-8 weeks) allow GH axis recovery. Second cycles often show similar responses to first cycles. Some users report diminishing returns by cycle 3-4.

What should I do during off-cycle?

Maintain training and nutrition. GH levels normalize within 1-2 weeks. Some users add testosterone support or other peptides during off-cycle to maintain gains.

Can I stack other peptides during sermorelin cycle?

Yes. CJC-1295, ipamorelin, and GHRP-6 stack synergistically. Avoid stacking multiple GHRH agonists; synergize with GHRP secretagogues instead.

How do I know if I'm responding to my cycle?

Sleep improvement (week 2-3), energy boost (week 3-4), strength gains (week 4+), and body composition changes (week 8+) indicate good response.
