GHRP-6 Cycle Guide

Length, Dosing & PCT Protocol

Standard GHRP-6 cycles are 8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off. Typical protocol: 100-200 mcg once or twice daily. Longer cycles (12-16 weeks) can be used but increase endogenous GH suppression risk. Off-cycle periods allow natural GH recovery and prevent tolerance. Advanced users employ micro-dosing or daily low-dose protocols for sustained results.

Standard GHRP-6 Cycling Protocol

The gold standard GHRP-6 cycle is 8-12 weeks on (continuous use), followed by 4-8 weeks off (no GHRP-6). This timing balances: maximum results (8+ weeks needed for significant lean mass gains), endogenous GH preservation (longer cycles suppress natural GH output), and practical compliance (3-month cycles are manageable; 6-month continuous use is challenging). Example protocol: 100 mcg GHRP-6 once daily for 10 weeks, then 4-6 weeks off. Or: 100-150 mcg twice daily for 8 weeks, then 4-8 weeks off. Post-cycle, endogenous GH recovers within 4-8 weeks, allowing the next cycle.

Extended Cycling: 12-16 Week On-Cycles

Some users run longer on-cycles (12-16 weeks):

  • Extended timeline allows 30-40% greater lean mass gains
  • Risk: greater suppression of endogenous GH output
  • Recovery period must extend to 8-12 weeks post-cycle
  • Only recommended for advanced users with experience
  • Benefit/risk ratio favors standard 8-12 week cycles for most users

Off-Cycle Recovery: Why Breaks Are Essential

Off-cycle breaks serve multiple functions: (1) Allow endogenous GH secretion to recover (baseline GH often suppressed 30-50% after 8+ weeks continuous GHRP-6); (2) Prevent receptor downregulation/desensitization (continuous GHS-R activation reduces cellular responsiveness); (3) Allow hypothalamic-pituitary function to reset; (4) Provide psychological break from injection routine. During off-cycles, lean mass is largely retained if training continues (75-85% retention common). Some fat may return if caloric intake is not maintained. Complete GH recovery requires 4-8 weeks.

Micro-Dosing and Daily Sustained Protocol

Advanced users employ alternative cycling approaches: Micro-dosing: 50 mcg daily (half standard dose) continuously. Benefits: sustained GH elevation with less receptor downregulation; reduced cortisol vs. higher doses; better endogenous GH preservation. Drawback: slower lean mass gains (30-40% slower than standard dosing). Daily sustained (365-day use): Some users inject GHRP-6 daily year-round at low doses (100 mcg once daily). Benefits: continuous results without off-cycle breaks. Drawbacks: high receptor desensitization risk, significant endogenous GH suppression, potential for pituitary adaptation. Most experts do not recommend continuous year-round GHRP-6.

Post-Cycle Management

Best practices after discontinuing GHRP-6:

  • Continue intense training (preserves lean mass)
  • Maintain high protein intake (0.8-1g per lb bodyweight)
  • Prioritize sleep (7-9 hours nightly for GH recovery)
  • Manage stress (high stress delays GH recovery)
  • Avoid extreme caloric deficit (promotes muscle loss)
  • Consider natural GH-supportive supplements (lysine, arginine, glycine)

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the minimum cycle length for results?

Results require minimum 6-8 weeks, but optimal results appear at 10-12 weeks. Cycles shorter than 6 weeks produce minimal lean mass gains.

Can I cycle GHRP-6 more frequently (e.g., 6 weeks on, 2 weeks off)?

Shorter cycles prevent adequate GH recovery. Standard 8-12 weeks on, 4-8 weeks off is optimal. Shorter cycles may reduce effectiveness over time.

Do I need to cycle if using low doses (50 mcg daily)?

Lower doses cause less GH suppression, so cycling is less critical. However, periodic breaks (e.g., 1 week off per month) help prevent desensitization.

What happens if I continue GHRP-6 past 12 weeks?

Continued use past 12 weeks increases endogenous GH suppression and receptor downregulation. Lean mass gains slow dramatically after week 12 regardless. Cycling is more effective.

How long until GH returns to normal after cycling off?

Endogenous GH typically recovers within 4-8 weeks of discontinuation. Marker hormone LH/FSH recovers faster (2-3 weeks); GH recovery is slower.

Can I stack another peptide during off-cycle?

Yes. Many users stack BPC-157 or TB-500 during off-cycles for recovery support without interfering with GH recovery.
