Dihexa side effects are rare and mild: occasional headaches, sleep disturbances (insomnia or vivid dreams), mild anxiety, and transient mood changes. Most resolve within 2-4 weeks as the nervous system adapts. No serious adverse events documented. Side effects are dose-dependent.
