What is the success rate?

Clinical trials: 40-50% of women with HSDD experience clinically meaningful improvement. Male efficacy similarly high. Non-responders represent 10-15%. Optimization of timing, dosing, and context improves outcomes.

Can PT-141 cause damage or addiction?

PT-141 is not addictive—no evidence of psychological or physical dependence. Damage risk is minimal with proper injection technique and cardiovascular monitoring. Most side effects are mild and transient.

Is PT-141 better than supplements?

PT-141 is clinically proven with strong efficacy; supplements are largely unproven. PT-141 mechanism is fully understood; supplement mechanisms are unclear. PT-141 is more expensive but far more effective for confirmed sexual dysfunction.

How long can I use PT-141?

Long-term safety data extends to 52 weeks in clinical trials. Longer-term data absent. PT-141 appears safe for ongoing use in appropriate patients; medical supervision recommended for extended protocols.

Will tolerance develop?
