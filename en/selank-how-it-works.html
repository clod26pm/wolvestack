Selank's mechanism combines multiple pathways: increases serotonin and dopamine via monoamine transporter modulation, enhances GABA signaling (neuronal inhibition), upregulates BDNF and NGF (neurotrophic factors), reduces pro-inflammatory cytokine IL-6, normalizes HPA axis (stress response system), and modulates oxytocin signaling (social/emotional processing).
