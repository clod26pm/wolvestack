Frequently Asked Questions

How much muscle can I gain on sermorelin?

4-6 lbs in 12 weeks for trained individuals (2-3 lbs for untrained). Results depend on training intensity, protein intake (must be 1.8+ g/kg), and caloric surplus.

What's the best training split on sermorelin?

Push/pull/legs (PPL) or upper/lower splits are optimal, hitting each muscle group 2x/week. Higher frequency (2x/week per muscle) synergizes with enhanced recovery from sermorelin.

Do I need a caloric surplus for muscle gain on sermorelin?

Yes. +300-500 kcal/day is recommended for hypertrophy. Sermorelin optimizes nutrient partitioning but doesn't create muscle in a deficit. Bulking on sermorelin is optimal.

How much protein do I need on sermorelin?

1.8-2.2 g/kg is optimal for muscle building. 1.6 g/kg is minimum. More than 2.2 g/kg provides minimal additional benefit.

Should I stack sermorelin with other muscle-building peptides?

Yes. Stacking with CJC-1295, ipamorelin, or GHRP-6 amplifies muscle gain by 25-50%. Avoid excessive stacking; risk of diminishing returns and side effects.

Can I build muscle without training on sermorelin?

Sermorelin amplifies muscle-building response to training but doesn't create muscle in absence of training stimulus. Training is mandatory for results.
