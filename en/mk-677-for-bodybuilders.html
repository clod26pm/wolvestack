Off-Season Bulking Protocol

MK-677 20-25 mg daily + testosterone 400-500 mg/week + Nandrolone 300-400 mg/week for 16-20 weeks. This is the bodybuilding gold-standard bulking stack. Expected results: 25-30 lbs lean mass gain, 25-40% strength increase, superior joint health. Use melatonin (1-3 mg) and optimize sleep for maximum GH elevation.

Managing Prolactin During Cycles

Triple stack (MK-677 + test + nandrolone) elevates prolactin significantly. Use Cabergoline 0.25 mg 2x weekly to manage. Monitor mood, libido, gynecomastia. Prolactin management is critical for long-term fertility and sexual function.

Pre-Contest Preparation

Discontinue MK-677 4-6 weeks before competition. MK-677 causes water retention and elevated prolactin both problematic for stage presentation. Switch to testosterone only or lower dose TRT. Dehydration and sodium manipulation are easier without MK-677 in system.

PCT and Recovery

Post-cycle: 8-10 week PCT with Clomiphene 50-100 mg daily + HCG 5,000 IU 2x weekly. Use MK-677 10-15 mg throughout PCT to preserve lean mass and support recovery. This is critical for the massive calories burned cycling down from offseason.

Nutrition for Maximum Gains

Caloric surplus: 500-750 calories above maintenance for maximum bulking. High protein (1.6-2.0 g/lb), consistent carbs (5-7 g/lb), healthy fats (0.4-0.6 g/lb). Meal frequency: 4-5 meals daily to manage appetite and hit targets.

FAQ: Bodybuilder Questions

Q: When should I start MK-677 in my prep?

A: 8-12 weeks out from competition peak, begin transition off compound (stop MK-677 4-6 weeks out).

Q: Can I use MK-677 during cutting/prep phases?

A: Not typically. Appetite increase makes deficit harder. Stick to on/off protocol.
