Topical GHK-Cu Dosage

Effective topical GHK-Cu concentration ranges 1-5%, with optimal range 2-3% for visible anti-aging results. Lower concentrations (1%) show minimal effects; higher concentrations (5%+) increase irritation risk without proportional benefit increase. Standard recommendation: start 2% concentration, apply daily (morning or night or both). For sensitive skin, start 1% and increase to 2% after 2 weeks if well-tolerated.

Application method: dispense 1-2 pumps of serum onto clean, slightly damp skin. Pat gently to absorb. On damp skin, peptide penetration improves compared to completely dry skin. Use daily for 8-12 weeks minimum to assess results. Consistent daily application is non-negotiable—sporadic use extends timeline and reduces final outcomes significantly.

Injectable GHK-Cu Dosage

Standard injectable dosing: 1-3mg subcutaneous injection per session, frequency 2-3 times weekly. Typical protocol: 2mg twice weekly = 4mg/week, or 1mg three times weekly = 3mg/week. Higher doses (3mg per injection, 3x weekly = 9mg/week) accelerate results slightly but increase injection site irritation risk and cost significantly.

Injection site: subcutaneous (under skin, not intramuscular) on abdomen, upper arm, or thigh. Rotate injection sites to prevent lipohypertrophy (localized fat accumulation). Reconstitute powder immediately before injection with sterile bacteriostatic water, using 1mL for standard 2-3mg doses (making 2-3mg/mL concentration).

Optimizing Dosage for Your Goals

For fine lines only (facial): Start topical 2% daily. This achieves excellent localized results without systemic effect. For comprehensive anti-aging: Start injectable 1-2mg twice weekly. Systemic distribution addresses whole-body skin quality. For fastest results: Combine topical (2% daily) + injectable (2mg 2x/week). This achieves results 2-4 weeks faster than either alone. For sensitive skin: Start topical 1% every other day, increase to daily after 2 weeks if tolerated.

Increasing Dosage Over Time

Start conservatively (topical 1-2%, injectable 1mg) and assess tolerance over 2 weeks. If well-tolerated and results are minimal by week 4, increase dose by 25-50%: topical to 3%, injectable to 1.5mg. Increasing too aggressively increases irritation risk without proportional benefit. Most users find optimal sweet spot at topical 2% or injectable 1-2mg.

Is More Always Better?

Doubling GHK-Cu dose doesn't double results. Collagen synthesis has natural ceiling—you can't force faster collagen production beyond skin's capacity. High-dose GHK-Cu mainly increases cost and irritation risk without dramatically better results. Sweet spot for most users is: topical 1-2% daily or injectable 1-2mg 2-3x weekly.

Adjusting Based on Results

If minimal improvement by week 6 at current dose, increase 25-50% for next cycle. If excellent results with mild irritation, consider reducing dose to improve tolerability. If no improvement after 12 weeks at high dose, reassess formulation quality—dosage isn't the limiting factor; likely product stability or inconsistent use.

FAQ: GHK Dosage

What GHK-Cu concentration should I start with?
Topical: 1-2%. Injectable: 1mg 2-3x weekly. Start conservative, increase after 2 weeks if needed.
Can I double my dose to get faster results?
Slightly, but not proportionally. Doubling dose accelerates results 1-2 weeks while increasing irritation. Standard dosing is usually optimal.
Is higher concentration always better?
No. 5%+ concentrations increase irritation without proportional benefit. 1-3% is optimal for most users.
How often should I inject GHK-Cu?
2-3x per week is standard. Less frequent (1x/week) extends timeline; more frequent (4+x/week) increases irritation without major benefit.
Can I apply topical GHK-Cu twice daily?
Yes, if using lower concentration (1-2%). Daily 2% is equivalent to 2x daily 1%. Higher concentrations 2x daily increase irritation risk significantly.
Should I increase dosage mid-cycle?
Only if minimal improvement by week 6. If results are progressing normally, maintain same dose through 12 weeks. Increasing mid-cycle increases irritation risk.

Summary

Standard GHK-Cu dosing: topical 1-2% daily or injectable 1-2mg 2-3x weekly. Start conservative, increase gradually if needed. Higher doses accelerate results slightly but increase irritation risk. Consistency matters more than dose—daily application beats higher concentration with sporadic use. Find your optimal sweet spot and maintain it through 12-week cycles.
