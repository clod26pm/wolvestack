Some users report mood improvement that may help with PMS-related emotional symptoms. However, it is not specifically designed for PMS treatment. Consider complementary approaches for specialized PMS management.

Insufficient safety data exists for pregnancy or breastfeeding. Recommend avoiding during these periods. Consult healthcare provider if considering use while pregnant or nursing.
