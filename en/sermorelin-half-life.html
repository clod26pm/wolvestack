Sermorelin Half-Life

Pharmacokinetics & Optimal Injection Timing

Medical Disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. Not FDA-approved for human use. Consult a licensed healthcare professional. See full disclaimer.

Sermorelin has an extremely short half-life (approximately 4 minutes in plasma), but its biological effects persist much longer (4-6 hours) due to GH secretion stimulation continuing after peptide clearance. Steady-state GH elevation is achieved by day 3-5 of daily injection. Tissue residence time of sermorelin itself is brief (<1 hour); the downstream GH pulse it triggers lasts 2-4 hours. This pharmacokinetic profile explains why daily injection is superior to less frequent dosing.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's sermorelin's actual half-life?

Plasma half-life is ~4 minutes. GH secretion it triggers lasts 2-4 hours. Biological effects persist 4-6 hours due to cascading hormonal responses.

How long before sermorelin reaches steady-state?

Daily injections achieve steady-state GH elevation by day 3-5. Optimal effects plateau around day 7-10 as the HPTA fully adapts.

Can I inject sermorelin every other day?

Yes, but results are ~20-30% smaller than daily dosing. The very short half-life means every-other-day administration misses off-days, reducing cumulative GH elevation.

Does sermorelin accumulate in tissues?

Sermorelin itself doesn't accumulate (4-min half-life), but downstream GH elevation becomes more pronounced with daily injection due to enhanced GH pulse amplitude and frequency.

Why inject in the evening instead of morning?

Evening injection aligns sermorelin's GH-stimulating effect with natural GH peaks during sleep, maximizing the anabolic window and improving sleep quality itself.

How long after injection does GH peak?

GH peaks 30-60 minutes after sermorelin injection. The peak lasts 2-4 hours. Subsequent GH pulses occur throughout the 24-hour period with daily injection.
