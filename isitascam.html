How it works

You give us the message. Our AI reads it the way a careful, scam-savvy friend would. In about 5 seconds you get one of three clear answers:

✓ Looks Safe
⚠ Be Careful
✕ Likely a Scam

Plus the specific red flags we found, why we flagged them, and exactly what to do next — should you reply, block, or report?

What we catch

  • Phishing emails (fake bank alerts, fake delivery notices, fake IRS)
  • Smishing — scam text messages
  • Romance scams
  • Tech support scams
  • Investment and crypto scams
  • Grandparent scams ("your grandkid is in jail")
  • Package delivery scams
  • Government impersonation scams
  • Prize and lottery scams
  • Charity impersonation
  • Fake invoice and refund scams
  • Job offer scams

Privacy you can trust

  • Your messages are never stored on our servers
  • Analysis happens in seconds, then it's forgotten
  • No tracking, no ad targeting, no data sales
  • Your messages are sent over HTTPS to our analysis proxy and forwarded to Anthropic's Claude API. Per Anthropic's API terms, content is never used for training.
