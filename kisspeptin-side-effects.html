Frequently Asked Questions

Is this treatment safe?

Yes. Minimal adverse events in clinical research spanning decades. No serious safety concerns documented. Excellent safety profile compared to alternative treatments.

How long until I see results?

Hormone response: 30 minutes. Fertility improvements: 2-6 weeks depending on baseline status. Full optimization: 8-12 weeks. Timeline varies individually.

Will results persist after stopping treatment?

Yes, depending on baseline. Reproductive axis improvements often sustain after discontinuation. No suppression to reverse. Benefits persist indefinitely in many cases.

Can I use this long-term?

Yes. Safe for indefinite use. No tolerance develops. No maximum treatment duration identified in research. Extended use well-tolerated and effective.

What about side effects?

Minimal: occasional headache, rare flushing, minimal injection site reactions. No serious adverse events. Very well tolerated compared to hormone alternatives.

How does this compare to hormone replacement?

Kisspeptin restores natural production (no suppression). Hormone replacement replaces function (causes suppression). Kisspeptin physiologically superior for long-term use and fertility preservation.
