## 🔍 Trend Report

### Top 3 Findings — March 17, 2026

**1. RFK Jr. Announces Reversal of FDA Peptide Ban (HIGHEST PRIORITY)**
On February 27, 2026, HHS Secretary RFK Jr. announced on the Joe Rogan Experience (Episode #2461) that approximately 14 of the 19 peptides placed on the FDA's Category 2 "do not compound" list in September 2023 will be moved back to Category 1 status. This would restore legal access via compounding pharmacies with a physician's prescription. BPC-157, GHK-Cu, and Thymosin Alpha-1 are among the likely returners. **Critically: as of mid-March 2026, no formal Federal Register notice has been published — this is announced intent, not finalized policy.** Industry analysts expect the formal reclassification within weeks. This is the single biggest peptide story in years and is dominating r/peptides, r/biohackers, and every wellness podcast in the space.

**2. New Systematic Review Validates BPC-157 for Musculoskeletal Healing**
A major peer-reviewed systematic review published in PMC (2025–2026) analyzed 544 articles spanning 1993–2024, ultimately including 36 studies. Findings show BPC-157 enhances growth hormone receptor expression, reduces inflammatory cytokines, promotes angiogenesis via VEGFR2/Akt-eNOS pathways, and improves functional, structural, and biomechanical outcomes in muscle, tendon, ligament, and bone injury models. Neurological applications (stroke, TBI, gut-brain axis) are an emerging research frontier. Combined BPC-157 + TB-500 ("Wolverine Stack") showed synergistic effects on muscle regeneration timelines in preclinical models. No large-scale human trials yet — but the preclinical foundation is the strongest it's ever been.

**3. FDA Approves Oral Wegovy — GLP-1 Peptide Enters New Era**
The FDA formally approved oral semaglutide (25 mg Wegovy pill) for weight management — the first GLP-1 pill cleared for obesity. Clinical trials showed 16.6% mean weight loss at 64 weeks with full adherence; one-third of adherent patients hit 20%+ weight loss. Simultaneously, a 52-week head-to-head trial found the novel oral GLP-1 orforglipron outperformed oral semaglutide on both A1c reduction and weight loss. The GLP-1 landscape is shifting fast — injectable to oral, and new entrants challenging Wegovy's dominance.
