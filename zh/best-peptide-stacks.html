The best peptide stacks combine complementary mechanisms: Wolverine Stack (BPC-157+TB-500) for injury recovery, GH Stack (CJC-1295+Ipamorelin) for growth hormone, Fat Loss Stack (AOD-9604+GHK-Cu), Longevity Stack (Epithalon+Humanin+MOTS-c), Anxiety Stack (Selank+BPC-157), and Immune Stack (Thymosin Alpha-1+LL-37). Why Stack Peptides?

Stacking pairs peptides with distinct mechanisms to achieve synergistic benefits—effects greater than either peptide alone. Complementary peptides can accelerate healing, enhance growth signaling, optimize fat loss, or improve mood through coordinated pathways. Effective stacking requires understanding mechanism complementarity, proper dosing, and ideally medical guidance.

The Wolverine Stack: BPC-157 + TB-500

The gold standard injury recovery stack combines BPC-157's collagen-synthesis and angiogenic effects with TB-500's myogenic enhancement for accelerated tissue repair.

Dosing

  • BPC-157: 250-500 mcg daily (SC or oral)
  • TB-500: 2-4 mg twice weekly SC
  • Duration: 8-12 weeks

Best For

ACL/MCL injuries, rotator cuff tears, tendinitis, ligament sprains, chronic pain syndromes.

The GH Stack: CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin

Maximizes growth hormone through dual GHRH and GHRP pathway stimulation, producing superior GH elevation and associated benefits.

Dosing

  • CJC-1295 (DAC): 2 mg weekly SC
  • Ipamorelin: 250-500 mcg once or twice daily SC
  • Duration: 8-16 weeks

Benefits

  • Muscle mass gains (0.5-1.5 kg monthly)
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Fat loss
  • Enhanced recovery
  • Skin elasticity improvement

The Fat Loss Stack: AOD-9604 + GHK-Cu

Targets body composition through lipolytic activation while preserving tissue quality and metabolic health during weight loss.

Dosing

  • AOD-9604: 300 mcg once or twice daily SC
  • GHK-Cu: 250 mcg daily
  • Duration: 8-12 weeks

Results

1-2 kg fat loss weekly (with diet/training), preserved muscle mass, improved skin elasticity, better metabolic efficiency.

The Longevity Stack: Epithalon + Humanin + MOTS-c

Addresses cellular aging through telomerase activation, mitochondrial protection, and metabolic optimization—a comprehensive longevity protocol.

Dosing

  • Epithalon: 10 mg daily (cycled: 10 days on/off)
  • Humanin: 250 mcg daily intranasal
  • MOTS-c: 250 mcg daily intranasal

The Anxiety Stack: Selank + BPC-157 + Semax

Comprehensive anxiolytic protocol combining direct mood regulation, cognitive enhancement, and gut-brain axis healing.

Dosing

  • Selank: 250-500 mcg once or twice daily
  • Semax: 250-500 mcg once or twice daily intranasal
  • BPC-157: 250-500 mcg daily

Stacking Principles

  • Use complementary mechanisms (avoid redundancy)
  • Introduce peptides individually first
  • Escalate doses gradually
  • Monitor subjective and objective effects
  • Consider cycling protocols for sensitivity maintenance
  • Seek medical guidance when possible

Frequently Asked Questions

Are peptide stacks safe?
Individual peptides have documented safety; stacking data is limited. Theoretical risks are minimal with complementary compounds. Medical oversight recommended.
How long before results?
Initial effects in 1-4 weeks; substantial benefits by week 8-12. Long-term markers require 12-16+ weeks.
Most cost-effective stack?
Wolverine Stack (BPC-157+TB-500) offers excellent cost-to-benefit for injury recovery.
Can I stack with other supplements?
Generally yes. No major interactions documented between peptides and standard supplements.
How do I know if it's working?
Track objective (pain scores, strength, body composition) and subjective (mood, energy, recovery) effects.
Can I create a custom stack?
Yes, if you understand mechanisms. Consulting a practitioner minimizes risk and optimizes outcomes.
