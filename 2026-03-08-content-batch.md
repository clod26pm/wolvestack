**MAJOR SHIFT: RFK Jr. FDA Peptide Reclassification (February 27, 2026)**

HHS Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr. announced that approximately 14 peptides previously categorized as FDA Category 2 "do not compound" will move to Category 1, allowing licensed compounding pharmacies to legally prepare them with physician prescriptions. Affected peptides include BPC-157, TB-500, GHK-Cu, CJC-1295, MOTS-C, Thymosin Alpha-1, and others. **Critical nuance:** Category 1 status means legal compounding pathway, not FDA approval or blanket safety endorsement. Formal FDA publication pending (as of early March 2026).
