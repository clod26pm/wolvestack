MOTS-c timeline: week 1-2 minimal change; week 3-4 energy increases noticeably; weeks 5-6 glucose improvements, visible fat loss beginning; weeks 7-8 aesthetic changes accelerate; weeks 9-12 dramatic transformation. Peak benefits weeks 8-12. Results persist 6-12 weeks post-cycle.

Week 1-2: Initial Phase

Minimal visible changes. Possible slight appetite decrease. Body adjusting to MOTS-c signaling. Steady-state plasma levels building. Users may feel 'off' temporarily - normal. Continue protocol, benefits emerge week 3.

Week 3-4: Energy and Early Benefits

Energy increases noticeably - users wake fresher, afternoon crashes disappear, training feels easier. Clothes fit looser despite minimal weight change (metabolic shift beginning). Some users report slightly improved mood and sleep. This phase is psychologically encouraging - motivation increases as benefits appear.

Week 5-6: Metabolic Improvements

If measuring fasting glucose, improvements appear (15-25% expected). Visible fat loss around midsection (1-2 inches waist reduction possible). Strength in gym improves noticeably - adds 5-10 lbs to major lifts. Recovery between sessions accelerates. Endurance improves - previously tiring activities feel easier.

Week 7-8: Aesthetic Changes

Dramatic aesthetic improvements become obvious. Abdominal definition appears; muscles more separated; overall body composition obviously improved. Weight loss continues 1.5-3 lbs weekly depending on deficit. Strength continues improving 5-10 lbs on major lifts. Users often feel motivation peaks - results visible and rewarding.

Week 9-12: Peak Benefits and Plateau

Continued improvements but rate slows slightly - peak benefits achieved. Body composition dramatically transformed. Strength and endurance at cycle maximum. Beyond week 12, diminishing returns emerge: benefits plateau while side effects may accumulate. Ideal stopping point for most cycles.

Post-Cycle Weeks 1-4

Water retention drops 2-3 lbs in days 1-5. Appetite normalizes. Strength retention excellent (80-90% of gains maintained). Energy dips slightly but normalizes by week 2. Sleep quality remains slightly improved. Benefits partially persist: metabolic improvements linger weeks 1-6.
