Kisspeptin for Beginners

Complete Starter Guide

📖 10 min read 🔬 Research & Education ⚠️ Not medical advice ✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Beginner guide: start 1 mcg/kg every 2 hours, progress to 90-120 min intervals by day 3-5. LH response 30 min; hormones normalize 1-3 days; meaningful fertility changes 2-4 weeks. Conservative approach minimizes side effects while maximizing results.

Getting Started: Conservative Protocol

Week 1: 1 mcg/kg every 2 hours. For 70 kg person = 70 mcg, 4-5 injections daily. Allows tolerance assessment. Monitor for headache or excessive stimulation. Week 2: Progress to 1-2 mcg/kg every 90-120 minutes (6-7 injections). Week 3+: Titrate to therapeutic range (2-3 mcg/kg) based on LH response.

Calculating Your Personal Dose

Formula: mcg/kg × bodyweight = injection dose. Example: 70 kg × 1 mcg/kg = 70 mcg per injection. Start at 1 mcg/kg conservative range. Increase by 0.5-1 mcg/kg every 3-5 days until LH doubles/decuples baseline (30 min post-injection).

First Week Expectations

Day 1-3: LH response 30 min post-injection validates administration. Energy slight improvement. Sleep possibly better. Day 4-7: Hormone changes measurable via bloodwork. Appetite increase (beneficial for muscle gain). Mild facial flushing possible (rare). Overall tolerability excellent.

Tracking Your Response

Get baseline bloodwork before starting: LH, FSH, testosterone (men), estrogen (women). Retest day 3-5 post-injection to confirm LH elevation. Test again 2 weeks in to validate hormone normalization. Women: ultrasound follicle monitoring if pursuing conception. Men: baseline semen analysis before starting.

Troubleshooting Common Beginner Issues

Non-response: Verify subcutaneous injection technique, confirm peptide quality, check refrigeration. If technique correct, increase dose. Excessive headache: Reduce dose by 0.5 mcg/kg, usually resolves in 24-48 hrs. Missed injections: Resume at next scheduled time; missed doses don't derail cycles.

Sleep Optimization During Treatment

Kisspeptin improves sleep quality, but requires 7-9 hours nightly for optimal hormone response. Prioritize bedtime consistency. Avoid stimulants 4-6 hours before bed. Many beginners report best results with 8:30pm bedtime, supporting melatonin/sleep hormone alignment.

Beginner-Specific Safety Considerations

No serious adverse events expected. Start conservative to assess individual tolerance. Stay hydrated (supports peptide processing). Maintain consistent meal timing (empty stomach for injections improves GH pulse response). Track symptoms: headache, flushing, energy, libido, sleep—all typically positive within 1-2 weeks.

When to Advance Dosing

Advance by 0.5-1 mcg/kg every 3-5 days IF: no adverse effects, LH response confirmed at current dose, seeking higher hormone elevation. STOP advancing IF: persistent headache despite 48-hr wait, excessive stimulation symptoms, or target hormone achieved. Typical therapeutic endpoint: 2-3 mcg/kg every 90-120 min.

Realistic Beginner Expectations

Hormones normalize: 1-3 days. Energy improvement: 3-7 days. Sleep quality: 1-2 weeks. Visible body composition (muscle/fat): 4-6 weeks. Fertility improvements: women 4-6 weeks (cycle restoration), men 8-12 weeks (sperm improvement). Set realistic timelines to maintain motivation.

FAQ

Should I start on weekday or weekend?

Start on a day allowing rest and observation—weekend ideal. Enables monitoring for side effects without work stress. Transition to normal schedule by day 3-5 once tolerance confirmed.

What if I'm nervous about injections?

Insulin syringes (25-31G) are small and minimally painful. Most users report forgetting they're injecting within 2-3 weeks. Start with abdomen (less nerve-dense) for minimal pain.

Can I exercise while starting kisspeptin?

Light activity fine day 1-2. Normal training by day 3+. Kisspeptin enhances recovery, making training more effective. No conflict.

How long before I see fertility improvements?

Women: ovulation within 1 week of consistent dosing; cycle normalization 4-6 weeks. Men: testosterone within 1-2 weeks; sperm improvement 8-12 weeks. Both: sexual function within 2-3 weeks.

Should I get bloodwork before starting?

Yes, baseline is recommended (LH, FSH, testosterone/estrogen, full panel). Provides before/after comparison, validates dose response, identifies baseline abnormalities requiring medical attention.

What if I miss a dose?

Resume at next scheduled time. Occasional missed doses (1-2 per week) don't meaningfully impact results. Consistency matters, but perfect adherence not required.
