Orexin-A Stacking

Synergistic Peptide Combinations and Protocols

📖 8 min read 🔬 Research & Education ⚠️ Not medical advice ✅ Last reviewed: April 2026

Medical Disclaimer

For informational and educational purposes only. Not FDA-approved for human use. Consult a licensed healthcare professional. See full disclaimer.

Orexin-A stacks effectively with growth hormone secretagogues (CJC-1295, GHRP-6), tissue-healing peptides (BPC-157, TB-500), and circadian-aligning peptides (melanotan II). Avoid combining with sedating peptides (DSIP, selank) or sleep-promoting compounds. Timing is critical: orexin-A morning, stacking partners evening (post-training or before sleep) for complementary effects.

Orexin-A + CJC-1295/GHRP-6 Stack

Rationale: Orexin provides all-day alertness and endurance; CJC-1295/GHRP-6 promote GH release, muscle growth, recovery. Synergy: enhanced training capacity (orexin-A) + enhanced recovery and growth (GH secretagogues). Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 100 mcg CJC-1295 + 100-200 mcg GHRP-6 post-training or before sleep. Timeline: 4-6 week cycles.

Orexin-A + TB-500 Stack

Rationale: Orexin supports endurance and energy; TB-500 promotes healing and tissue repair. Synergy: enhanced training volume tolerance + accelerated recovery from microtrauma. Benefit: athletes can sustain higher training volume without overuse injury accumulation. Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 2-5 mg TB-500 twice weekly. Duration: continuous or periodic 4-6 week blocks.

Orexin-A + BPC-157 Stack

Rationale: Orexin-A supports wakefulness and training capacity; BPC-157 promotes tissue healing and GI protection. Synergy: sustained training intensity without injury risk, improved GI function despite high exercise volume. Benefit: particularly valuable for endurance athletes. Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 200-500 mcg BPC-157 once or twice daily.

Orexin-A + Melanotan II Stack

Rationale: Orexin-A provides wakefulness; melanotan II promotes circadian alignment and sexual function/libido. Synergy: complementary effects on energy, motivation, and libido. Timing is critical: melanotan II afternoon/evening (aligns circadian rhythm), orexin-A morning (promotes alertness). Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 0.5-1 mg melanotan II late afternoon.

Orexin-A + HCG Stack (Male Hormone Support)

Rationale: Orexin-A supports energy and training; HCG maintains testosterone/fertility during extended training blocks. Synergy: sustained energy + maintained hormonal health. Benefit: athletes using other suppressive protocols benefit from HCG stabilization. Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 250-500 IU HCG daily or every other day.

Orexin-A + Ipamorelin Stack

Rationale: Orexin-A for daytime energy and cognition; ipamorelin for GH release and recovery. Synergy: sustained intensity + enhanced recovery. Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 100-300 mcg ipamorelin evening (post-training or before sleep). Both can be combined with GHRP-6 for additive GH stimulation.

Orexin-A + Humanin Stack

Rationale: Orexin-A supports metabolic arousal and performance; humanin provides cytoprotection and metabolic support. Synergy: enhanced endurance + cellular protection from training stress. Benefit: particularly valuable for older or metabolically compromised individuals. Dosing: 1-2 mg orexin-A morning, 5-15 mg humanin daily.

Incompatible Combinations to Avoid

DSIP (delta sleep-inducing peptide): directly antagonizes orexin signaling, reduces wakefulness benefits. Selank: sedating properties conflict with orexin-A. Benzodiazepines or sedating sleep aids: antagonize orexin-A effects. High-dose melatonin (late evening after morning orexin): can suppress remaining orexin activity. Avoid these combinations; they reduce efficacy of both compounds.

Timing Strategy for Stacked Protocols

Morning (6-10 AM): orexin-A injection (all-day wakefulness). Afternoon (4-6 PM, if needed): second dose of orexin-A (only if longer-duration effects needed; skip 1-2 days/week to prevent tolerance). Post-training or evening (6-10 PM): growth hormone secretagogues, tissue-healing peptides, hormonal support compounds. This timing allows complementary benefits without antagonism.

Cycling Stacked Protocols

4-6 week cycles of full stack on, followed by 1 week rest or orexin-A only. This prevents cumulative tolerance and allows recovery. Some athletes prefer 5-6 days/week stacking with 1-2 days/week rest. Year-round stacking (no off-season) is safe but requires periodic dose resets or cycling.

Cost and Complexity Considerations

Adding stacking partners increases cost 1.5-3x compared to orexin-A alone. Complexity increases (multiple injections, timing coordination). Value increases substantially for serious athletes or those with specific goals (muscle gain, injury recovery). For casual users, orexin-A alone is sufficient and more cost-effective.

Women-Specific Stacking Considerations

Women typically use lower orexin-A doses (0.5-1.5 mg) and benefit from lower partner doses correspondingly. Hormonal stacking (HCG, etc.) is generally not appropriate for women. Focus stacks: orexin-A + BPC-157 (tissue health, training capacity) or orexin-A + TB-500 (healing, endurance).
