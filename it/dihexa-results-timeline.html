Dihexa produces rapid molecular changes (synaptogenesis within hours) but gradual cognitive improvements: subtle effects by week 1, noticeable memory gains weeks 2-4, peak cognitive benefits weeks 6-8, then plateau. Benefits often persist 4-12 weeks after cessation due to structural brain changes.
